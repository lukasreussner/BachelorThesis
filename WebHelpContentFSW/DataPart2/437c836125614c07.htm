HCOPy:PAGE:MARGin<device>:UNIT <Unit>

This command defines the unit in which the margins for the printout page are configured.

Suffix: 
<device>
1 | 2

Printing device.

Parameters:
<Unit>
MM | IN
MM

millimeters

IN

inches

*RST:MM
Example: HCOP:PAGE:MARG2:BOTT 2
Manual operation: See "Margins"
