CALCulate<n>:STATistics:SCALe:Y:UNIT <Unit>

This command selects the unit of the y-axis.

Suffix: 
<n>

Window

Parameters:
<Unit>
PCT | ABS
*RST:ABS
Example: CALC:STAT:SCAL:Y:UNIT PCT

Sets the percentage scale.
