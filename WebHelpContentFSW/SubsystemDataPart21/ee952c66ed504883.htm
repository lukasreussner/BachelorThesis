DOCSIS measurements (R&S FSW-K192/-K193) > Remote Commands for DOCSIS 3.1 Measurements > Configuring the DOCSIS 3.1 I/Q Measurement (Modulation Accuracy) > Tracking and Channel Estimation > SENSe:TRACking:PHASe
SENSe:TRACking:PHASe <State>

Activates or deactivates the compensation for phase drifts. If activated, the measurement results are compensated for phase drifts on a per-symbol basis.

Parameters:
<State>
ON | OFF | 0 | 1
*RST:1
Example: SENS:TRAC:PHAS ON
Example: 

For a detailed example see "Measurement 1: Measuring Modulation Accuracy".

Manual operation: See "Phase Tracking (downstream only)"
