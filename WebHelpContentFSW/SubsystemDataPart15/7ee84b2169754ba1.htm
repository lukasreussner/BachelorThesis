[SENSe:]CDPower:QINVert <State>

This command inverts the Q component of the signal.

Parameters:
ON | OFF | 1 | 0
*RST:0
Example: CDP:QINV ON

Activates inversion of Q component.

Manual operation: See "Invert Q"
