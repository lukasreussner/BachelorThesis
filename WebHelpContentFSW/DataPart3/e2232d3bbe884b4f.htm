[SENSe:]BANDwidth[:RESolution]:AUTO <State>

This command couples and decouples the resolution bandwidth to the span.

Parameters:
<State>
ON | OFF | 0 | 1
*RST:1
Example: BAND:AUTO OFF

Switches off the coupling of the resolution bandwidth to the span.
