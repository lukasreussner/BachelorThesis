IF Fixed

Defines a fixed intermediary frequency (IF) for measurements on frequency-converting DUTs with a fixed IF.

After you have defined the intermediary frequency, the application updates the frequency list accordingly.

Remote command: 

[SENSe:​]CONFigure:​MODE:​SYSTem:​IF:​FREQuency
