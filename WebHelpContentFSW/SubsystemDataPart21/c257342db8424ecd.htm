Profile

One of the active profiles defined in the "Profile List", which is assigned to the selected codeword.

For zero-bit loaded codewords, assign the profile "Unused".

Remote command: 

CONFigure:​DS:​CHANnel:​FCONfig<cwi>:​PROFile
