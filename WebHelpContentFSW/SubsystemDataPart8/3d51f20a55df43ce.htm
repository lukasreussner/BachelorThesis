[SENSe:]CONFigure:CONTrol <Mode>

This command selects the measurement mode for the hot and cold power measurements.

Note that selecting a noise source with resistor characteristics with [SENSe:]CORRection:ENR:CALibration:TYPE or [SENSe:]CORRection:ENR[:MEASurement]:TYPE automatically selects manual measurement mode.

Parameters:
<Mode>
AUTO

Performs the Power (Hot) and Power (Cold) measurement in one step.

MANual

Performs the Power (Hot) and Power (Cold) measurement in two separate steps.

Example: CONF:CONT AUTO

Selects the automatic measurement mode.

Manual operation: See "Meas Mode (Auto Manual)"
