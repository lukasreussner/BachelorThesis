Specific Measurement Settings

The "Power" settings, for example, determine which part of the hop/chirp is used to calculate the average power in one hop/chirp. The "Frequency/Phase" settings determine which part of the hop/chirp is used to calculate the average frequency/phase in one hop/chirp.

For details on the measurement range parameters see "Measurement Range".
