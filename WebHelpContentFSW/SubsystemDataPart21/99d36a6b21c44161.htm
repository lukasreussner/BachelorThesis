Power Unit

Determines whether the power results in the Result Summary and Signal Content Detailed are calculated as absolute values or relative to the power measured in the data subcarriers.

For absolute values, the unit depends on the "Unit" setting in the amplitude settings.

Note that in the Signal Content Detailed table, all power values use the same unit (as defined here), whereas in the Result Summary, the total power of the OFDM channel and the power measured in the 6-MHz channel containing the PLC are always provided as absolute values, regardless of the "Power Unit" setting.

Remote command: 

UNIT:​POWer
