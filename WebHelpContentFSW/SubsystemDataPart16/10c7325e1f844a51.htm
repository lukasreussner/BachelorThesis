Reference: Supported Bandclasses

The bandclass defines the frequency band used for ACLR and SEM measurements. It also determines the corresponding limits and ACLR channel settings according to the 1xEV-DO standard. The used bandclass is defined in the SEM or ACLR measurement settings (see "Bandclass").
