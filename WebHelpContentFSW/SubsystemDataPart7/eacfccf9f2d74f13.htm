Amplifier Measurements (R&S FSW-K18) > Remote Control Commands for Amplifier Measurements > Configuring Amplifier Measurements > Estimating and Compensating Signal Errors > Error Estimation and Compensation > CONFigure:SIGNal:ERRor:ESTimation:IQIMbalance[:STATe]
CONFigure:SIGNal:ERRor:ESTimation:IQIMbalance[:STATe] <State>

This command turns estimation of the I/Q Imbalance on and off.

Parameters:
<State>
ON | OFF | 1 | 0
*RST:1
Example: CONF:SIGN:ERR:EST:IQIM ON

Turns on error estimation.
