CONFigure:DS:CHANnel:CPES<i>:SUBCarrier:STOP <SCStop>

Defines the last subcarrier in a series of subcarriers to be configured identically in the "Continuous Pilots and Excluded Subcarrier Assignment" table.

Suffix: 
<i>
1..n

index in the "Continuous Pilots and Excluded Subcarrier Assignment" table

Parameters:
<SCStop>
integer

Subcarrier number

Must be higher than the parameter used by CONFigure:​DS:​CHANnel:​CPES<i>:​SUBCarrier:​STARt.

Range: 1  to  4K mode: 4095; 8K mode: 8191
Example: CONF:DS:CHAN:CPES2:SUBC:STOP 250
Example: 

For a detailed example see "Measurement 1: Measuring Modulation Accuracy".

Manual operation: See "Subcarrier Range(Start/ Increment/ Stop)"
