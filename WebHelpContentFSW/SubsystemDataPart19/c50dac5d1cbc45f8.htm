Demodulation

Access: "Overview" > "Demodulation"

Or: [MEAS CONFIG] > "Demod."

The demodulation settings define which PPDUs are to be analyzed, thus they define a logical filter.

The available demodulation settings vary depending on the selected digital standard in the "Signal Description" (see "Standard").
