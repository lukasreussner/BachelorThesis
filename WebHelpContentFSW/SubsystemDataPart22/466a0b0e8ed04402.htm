Defining MBSFN Characteristics

Access: "Overview" > "Signal Description" > "MBSFN Settings"

The MBSFN settings contain settings to configure Multimedia Broadcast Single Frequency Networks (MBSFNs).

The positioning reference signal settings are part of the "MBSFN Settings" tab of the "Signal Description" dialog box.
