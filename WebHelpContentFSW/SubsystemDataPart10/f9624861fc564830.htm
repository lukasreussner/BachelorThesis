Detection Mode

Indicates how the frequencies in the table were detected.

This information is useful in order to delete all manually defined or all measured (imported) frequencies in the table in one step (see "Remove Measured" / "Remove Manual").

"Entered"

Frequency settings were entered manually by the user.

"Measured"

Frequency settings were imported from the results of a previous wide search measurement (see "Spur Table")

Remote command: 

[SENSe:​]DIRected:​SAVE
