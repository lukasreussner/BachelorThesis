[SENSe:]POWer:ACHannel:SLOT:STOP <StopSlot>

Sets the last slot of the measurement.

Parameters:
<StopSlot>

The stop slot may not be lower than the start slot.

In the UE application, stop slots other than 1 require an external trigger. The default value is 1.

Range: 1 to 7
*RST:6
Example: POW:ACH:SLOT:STOP 5
