Noise Figure Measurements (R&S FSW-K30) > Remote Control Commands for Noise Figure Measurements > Defining the Measurement Frequency > [SENSe:]FREQuency:SINGle:COUPling
[SENSe:]FREQuency:SINGle:COUPling <State>

This command couples or decouples the frequency to the contents of the sweep list.

Parameters:
<State>
ON | 1

Only frequencies in the frequency list can be selected for single frequency measurements.

OFF | 0

Any frequency can be defined for single frequency measurements.

*RST:0
Example: FREQ:SING:COUP ON

Couples single frequency measurements to the frequency list.

Manual operation: See "Coupled to Sweep List"
