[SENSe:][LTE:]UL:DEMod:SISYnc <State>

This command turns suppressed interference synchronization on and off.

Parameters:
<State>
ON | OFF
*RST:OFF
Example: UL:DEM:SISY ON

Turns on suppressed interference synchronization.

Manual operation: See "Suppressed Interference Synchronization"
