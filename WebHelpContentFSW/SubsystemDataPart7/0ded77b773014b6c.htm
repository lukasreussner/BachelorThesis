[SENSe:]SWEep:TIME:AUTO <State>

This command turns automatic selection of an appropriate capture time on and off.

When you turn on this feature, the application calculates an appropriate capture time based on the reference signal and adjusts the other acquisition settings accordingly.

Parameters:
<State>
ON | OFF | 1 | 0
*RST:1
Example: SWE:TIME:AUTO ON

Selects automatic adjustment of the capture time.

Manual operation: See "Automatic adjustment"
