Results to check power characteristics

Power In

Signal power at the DUT input.

FETCh:​POWer:​INPut:​CURRent[:​RESult]?

Power Out

Signal power at the DUT output.

This result is calculated over the evaluation range only.

FETCh:​POWer:​OUTPut:​CURRent[:​RESult]?

Gain

Average gain calculated over all samples of the Gain Compression trace.

FETCh:​POWer:​GAIN:​CURRent[:​RESult]?

Crest Factor In

Crest factor of the signal at the DUT input. The crest factor is the ratio of the RMS and peak power.

FETCh:​POWer:​CFACtor:​IN:​CURRent[:​RESult]?

Crest Factor Out

Crest factor of the signal at the DUT output. The crest factor is the ratio of the RMS and peak power.

FETCh:​POWer:​CFACtor:​OUT:​CURRent[:​RESult]?

AM/AM Curve Width

Vertical spread of the samples in the AM/AM result display.

The spread is measured at the RMS level of the signal.

FETCh:​AMAM:​CWIDth:​CURRent[:​RESult]?

AM/PM Curve Width

Vertical spread of the samples in the AM/PM result display.

The spread is measured at the RMS level of the signal.

FETCh:​AMPM:​CWIDth:​CURRent[:​RESult]?

Compression Point (1 dB / 2 dB / 3 dB)

Input power where the gain deviates by 1 dB, 2 dB or 3 dB from a reference gain (see "Configuring compression point calculation").

In the graphical result, the compression points are indicated by horizontal red lines.

FETCh:​POWer:​P1DB:​CURRent[:​RESult]?

FETCh:​POWer:​P2DB:​CURRent[:​RESult]?

FETCh:​POWer:​P3DB:​CURRent[:​RESult]?
