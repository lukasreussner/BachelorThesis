Amplifier Measurements (R&S FSW-K18) > Remote Control Commands for Amplifier Measurements > Configuring Amplifier Measurements > Applying Digital Predistortion > CONFigure:DDPD[:STATe]
CONFigure:DDPD[:STATe] <State>

This command selects the type of DPD.

Parameters:
<State>
ON | 1

Selects direct DPD.

OFF | 0

Selects polynomial DPD.

*RST:OFF
Example: 

//Select direct DPD

CONF:DDPD ON
