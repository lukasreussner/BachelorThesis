NB-IoT Downlink (R&S FSW-K106) > Remote Control > Remote Commands to Configure the Application > Configuring I/Q Measurements > Demodulation > [SENSe:][LTE:]DL:DEMod:MCFilter
[SENSe:][LTE:]DL:DEMod:MCFilter <State>

This command turns suppression of interfering neighboring carriers on and off (e.g. LTE, WCDMA, GSM etc).

Parameters:
<State>
ON | OFF | 1 | 0
*RST:OFF
Example: 

//Turn on interference suppression

DL:DEM:MCF ON
Manual operation: See "Multicarrier Filter"
