CONFigure:CDPower[:BTS]:PVTime:SPOint <numeric value>

This command defines the switching point between uplink and downlink slots.

Parameters:
<numeric value>

1 to 7

*RST:3
Example: CONF:CDP:PVT:SPO 7

Sets the switching point to 7.
