[SENSe:]SWEep:TIME:AUTO <State>

This command couples and decouples the sweep time to the span and the resolution and video bandwidths.

Parameters:
<State>
ON | OFF | 0 | 1
*RST:1
Example: SWE:TIME:AUTO ON

Activates automatic sweep time.
