Amplifier Measurements (R&S FSW-K18) > Remote Control Commands for Amplifier Measurements > Configuring Amplifier Measurements > Configuring the Data Capture > [SENSe:]SWAPiq
[SENSe:]SWAPiq <State>

This command inverts the I and Q branches of the signal.

Parameters:
<State>
ON | OFF | 1 | 0
Example: SWAP ON

Inverts the I and Q channel.

Manual operation: See "Inverting the I/Q branches"
