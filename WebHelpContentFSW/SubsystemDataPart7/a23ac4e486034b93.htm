[SENSe:]POWer:ACHannel:BANDwidth:ALTernate<ch> <Bandwidth>

This command defines the channel bandwidth of the alternate channels.

Suffix: 
<ch>
1 to 11

Alternate channel number

Parameters:
<Bandwidth>
Range: 100 Hz  to  1000 MHz
*RST:14 kHz
