Phase Tracking

Activates or deactivates the compensation for phase drifts. If activated, the measurement results are compensated for phase drifts based on data symbol blocks (=512 symbols).

Tip: the phase drifts which will be used for compensation are displayed in the "Phase Tracking vs Symbol" result display.

Remote command: 

SENSe:​TRACking:​PHASe
