Limit Checking

Activates or deactivates limit checks globally for all adjacent and gap channels. In addition to this setting, limits must be defined and activated individually for each channel.

The results of the power limit checks are also indicated in the STAT:QUES:ACPL status registry (see "STATus:QUEStionable:ACPLimit Register").

Remote command: 

CALCulate<n>:​LIMit<k>:​ACPower[:​STATe]
