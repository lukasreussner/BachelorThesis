Gain Imbalance vs Carrier

Displays the minimum, average and maximum gain imbalance versus carrier in individual traces. For details on gain imbalance, see "Gain Imbalance".

Remote command: 

LAY:ADD? '1',RIGH,GAIN, see LAYout:​ADD[:​WINDow]?

Or:

CONFigure:​BURSt:​GAIN:​GCARrier[:​IMMediate]

Querying results:

TRACe[:​DATA], see "Error Vs Carrier"
