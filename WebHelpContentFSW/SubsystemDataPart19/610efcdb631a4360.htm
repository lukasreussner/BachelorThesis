CALCulate<n>:MARKer<m>:FUNCtion:POWer:RESult:MAXHold

Returns a comma-separated list of the maxhold trace results for power measurements.

Suffix: 
<n>, <m>

irrelevant

Return values: 
<Results>
<Power of main channel>, <Power of lower adjacent channel>,<Power of upper adjacent channel>,<Power of lower alternate adjacent channel 1>,<Power of upper alternate adjacent channel 1>,<Power of lower alternate adjacent channel 2>,<Power of upper alternate adjacent channel 2>
