Gate Range Definition for APD and CCDF

Access: "Overview" > "Select Measurement" > "APD"/"CCDF" > "APD Config"/ "CCDF Config" > "Edit Gate Ranges"

You can configure gate ranges for gated triggering in statistical measurements.

For background information on defining gate ranges see "APD and CCDF Basics - Gated Triggering".

The remote commands required to perform these tasks are described in "Using Gate Ranges for Statistical Measurements".

Up to three ranges can be defined for each of the six available traces.
