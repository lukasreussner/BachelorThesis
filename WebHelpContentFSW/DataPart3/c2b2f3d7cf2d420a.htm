Display and Limit Lines

Display and limit lines help you analyze a measurement trace.

Display lines are only available in the Spectrum and (optional) Analog Demodulation applications.

In the I/Q Analyzer application, lines are only available for measurements in the frequency domain.

Access: "Overview" > "Analysis" > "Lines"

For remote operation, see "Configuring Display and Limit Lines".
