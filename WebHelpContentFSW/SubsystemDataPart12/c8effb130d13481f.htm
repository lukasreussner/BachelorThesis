Message: Pattern Not (Entirely) Within Result Range

A pattern can only be found if it is entirely within the result range. Therefore, this error message always occurs with a "Pattern Waveform Not Found" error.

Solution: Choose the pattern as reference of your result range alignment. Then, the pattern will always be part of your result range and the pattern search can succeed.
