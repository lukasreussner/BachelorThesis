Carrier Axes Unit

For result displays that evaluate a parameter per carrier (e.g. "MER vs Carrier", "Group Delay", or "Spectrum Flatness"), you can define whether the carrier number or the carrier frequency (in Hz) is displayed on the x-axis. Note, however, that this setting applies to ALL result displays based on carriers.

Remote command: 

UNIT:​CAXes
