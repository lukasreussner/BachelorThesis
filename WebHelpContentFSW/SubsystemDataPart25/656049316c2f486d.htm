Power NB-IoT Carrier

Selects the way that the limits for the spectrum emission mask are calculated.

  • "Auto"
    The limit values are relative values based on the power of the NB-IoT carrier measured in the reference range.
  • "Manual"
    You can enter the power of the NB-IoT carrier manually. The limit values are absolute values based on the power of the NB-IoT carrier that you enter in the "Value" field.
Remote command: 

Mode: [SENSe:​]POWer:​SEM:​PIOM

Power: [SENSe:​]POWer:​SEM:​PIOV
