Distortion Max Frequency

Defines the upper frequency limit for most total harmonic distortion measurements. Only harmonics frequencies not exceeding this value are included in the THD calculation. The maximum allowed value is half the defined "Demodulation Bandwidth".

The setting has no effect on K2 and K3 or FM distortion results.
