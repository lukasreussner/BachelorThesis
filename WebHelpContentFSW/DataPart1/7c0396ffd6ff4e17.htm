[SENSe:]NPRatio:NOTCh<notch>:BWIDth:RELative <arg0>

Defines the bandwidth of the individual notch as a percentage of the defined channel frequency.

Suffix: 
<notch>
1..n
Parameters:
<arg0>
Range: 0  to  100
*RST:5
Example: NPR:CHAN:BWID 100000000
NPR:NOTC2:BWID:REL 5

Defines a notch bandwidth of 5% of channel bandwidth (100 MHz) = 5 MHz

Manual operation: See "Notch Bandwidth (Absolute / Relative to Channel BW)"
