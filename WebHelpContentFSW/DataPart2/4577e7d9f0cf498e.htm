Notch Bandwidth (Absolute / Relative to Channel BW)

Defines the bandwidth of the individual notch either as an absolute value, or as a percentage of the defined "Channel Bandwidth".

Remote command: 

[SENSe:​]NPRatio:​NOTCh<notch>:​BWIDth[:​ABSolute]

[SENSe:​]NPRatio:​NOTCh<notch>:​BWIDth:​RELative
