Channels and Carriers

In an OFDM system such as WLAN, the channel is divided into carriers using FFT / IFFT. Depending on the channel bandwidth, the FFT window varies between 64 and 512 (see also "Demodulation Parameters - Logical Filters"). Some of these carriers can be used (active carriers), others are inactive (e.g. guard carriers at the edges). The channel can then be determined using the active carriers as known points; inactive carriers are interpolated.
