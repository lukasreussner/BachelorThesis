[SENSe:]SWEep:EGATe <State>

This command turns gated measurements on and off.

In case of measurements with an external gate, the measured values are recorded as long as the gate is opened. During a sweep the gate can be opened and closed several times. The synchronization mechanisms with *OPC, *OPC? and *WAI remain completely unaffected.

The measurement ends when a particular number of measurement points has been recorded.

See "(Measurement) Points".

Parameters:
<State>
ON | OFF | 1 | 0
*RST:0
Example: SWE:EGAT ON

Switches on the external gate mode.

SWE:EGAT:TYPE EDGE

Switches on the edge-triggered mode.

SWE:EGAT:HOLD 100US

Sets the gate delay to 100 μs.

SWE:EGAT:LEN 500US

Sets the gate opening time to 500 μs.

INIT;*WAI

Starts a sweep and waits for its end.
