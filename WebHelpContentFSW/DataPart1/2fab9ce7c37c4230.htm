Percent Marker (CCDF only)

Defines a probability value. Thus, the power which is exceeded with a given probability can be determined very easily. If marker 1 is deactivated, it is switched on automatically.

For details on the results of the CCDF measurement see "APD and CCDF Results".

Remote command: 

CALCulate<n>:​MARKer<m>:​Y:​PERCent
