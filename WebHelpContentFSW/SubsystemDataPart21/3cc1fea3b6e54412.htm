Center Frequency

Defines the center frequency of the signal in Hertz.

The center frequency of the complete signal depends on the center frequency of the subcarrier 0 of the OFDM channel (fsc0), which defines the beginning of the OFDM spectrum. If the "OFDM Spectrum Location" is changed, then the general center frequency is also changed, and vice versa.

Remote command: 

[SENSe:​]FREQuency:​CENTer
