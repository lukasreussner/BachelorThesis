Highlight Symbols

Access: "Overview" > "Display Config" > "More"

If enabled, the symbol instants are highlighted as squares in the window for measured and reference signals in time (normal) display, and error displays.

Only evaluations that are based on symbols (e.g. constellations or traces, not eye diagrams) support this function.

Remote command: 

DISPlay[:​WINDow<n>]:​TRACe<t>:​SYMBol
