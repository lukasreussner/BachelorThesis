Unit

The R&S FSW measures the signal voltage at the RF input.

In the default state, the level is displayed at a power level of 1 mW (= dBm). Via the known input impedance (50 Ω or 75 Ω, see "Impedance"), conversion to other units is possible.

The following units are available and directly convertible:
  • dBm
  • dBmV
  • dBμV
  • dBμA
  • dBpW
  • Volt
  • Ampere
  • Watt
