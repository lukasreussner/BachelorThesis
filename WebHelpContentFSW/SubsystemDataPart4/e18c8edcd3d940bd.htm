Slot State (On/Off)

Activates or deactivates the selected slot. The R&S FSW GSM application expects an input signal within the active slots only.

At least the "Slot to Measure" must be active in order to evaluate it.

Remote command: 

CONFigure[:​MS]:​CHANnel:​SLOT<Number>[:​STATe]
