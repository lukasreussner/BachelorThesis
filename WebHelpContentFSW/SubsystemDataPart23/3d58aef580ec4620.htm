Spectrum Flatness SRS

The Spectrum Flatness SRS display shows the amplitude of the channel transfer function based on the sounding reference signal.

The measurement is evaluated over the currently selected slot in the currently selected subframe. The slot and subframe selection may be changed in the general settings.

Remote command: 

Selecting the result display: LAY:ADD ? '1',LEFT,SFSR

Querying results:

TRACe:DATA?

TRACe<n>[:​DATA]:​X?
