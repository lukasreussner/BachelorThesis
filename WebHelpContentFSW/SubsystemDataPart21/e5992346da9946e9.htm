[SENSe:]SYMBol:SIZE <Size>

Defines the size of the individual symbol points in the "Constellation" diagram.

Parameters:
<Size>
S1 | S2 | S3
S1

One symbol point is displayed by 1 pixel on the x-axis and 1 pixel on the y-axis

S2

One symbol point is displayed by 2 pixels on the x-axis and 2 pixels on the y-axis

S3

One symbol point is displayed by 3 pixels on the x-axis and 3 pixels on the y-axis

*RST:S1
Example: SENS:SYMB:SIZE S2
Manual operation: See "Symbol Points Size"
