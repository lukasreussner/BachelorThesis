Oscilloscope Sample Rate

Determines whether the R&S FSW-B2000 option uses the 10 GHz mode (default) or 20 GHz mode of the connected oscilloscope. The 20 GHZ mode achieves a higher decimation gain, but reduces the record length by half.

The R&S FSW-B5000 option always uses the 20 GHZ mode.

Remote command: 

SYSTem:​COMMunicate:​RDEVice:​OSCilloscope:​SRATe
