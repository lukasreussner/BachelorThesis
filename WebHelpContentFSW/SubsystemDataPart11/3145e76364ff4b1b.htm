Measurement Bandwidth

The measurement bandwidth and "Sample Rate" are interdependent and define the range of data to be captured. For information on supported sample rates and bandwidths see the data sheet. All bandwidth extension options up to 5 GHz are supported.

For details see "Data Acquisition".

Note: Data acquisition in MSRT mode. By default, the R&S FSW Transient Analysis application uses the largest possible measurement bandwidth. Depending on which bandwidth extension options are installed (if any), this may be up to 5 GHz. However, in MSRT mode a maximum of 160 MHz bandwidth is available. Thus, you must ensure the measurement bandwidth for Transient Analysis is available in MSRT mode. Otherwise you will not obtain useful results.

Remote command: 

[SENSe:​]BWIDth:​DEMod
