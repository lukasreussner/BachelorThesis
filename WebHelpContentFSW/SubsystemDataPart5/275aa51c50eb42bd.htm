K3

Relative amplitude of an AF signal's third harmonic, calculated as:

<amplitude of third harmonic> / <amplitude of fundamental>

The unit depends on the "Distortion" setting.

Remote command: 

CALC:AVI:THD:K3? '30'

CALC:AVI:THD:K3? '30FM'

CALC:AVI:THD:K3? '9960'

CALC:AVI:THD:K3? 'ID'
