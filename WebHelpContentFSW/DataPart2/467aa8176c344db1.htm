Technology Used for Transmission

The technology used for transmission by the individual channel can be defined for each channel. The required channel bandwidth and use of a weighting filter are preconfigured automatically according to the selected technology standard.

"GSM"

Transmission according to GSM standard

"W-CDMA"

Transmission according to W-CDMA standard

"LTE_xxx"

Transmission according to LTE standard for different channel bandwidths

"USER"

User-defined transmission; no automatic preconfiguration possible
