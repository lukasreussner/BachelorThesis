Starting a Measurement

When a DOCSIS 3.1 measurement channel is activated on the R&S FSW, a DOCSIS 3.1 I/Q measurement (Modulation Accuracy, see "DOCSIS 3.1 I/Q Measurement"), is started immediately. However, you can stop and start a new measurement any time.

Furthermore, you can perform a sequence of measurements using the Sequencer (see "Multiple Measurement Channels and Sequencer Function").

List of Commands
ABORt
INITiate<n>:CONMeas
INITiate<n>:CONTinuous
INITiate<n>[:IMMediate]
INITiate:REFResh
INITiate<n>:SEQuencer:ABORt
INITiate<n>:SEQuencer:IMMediate
INITiate<n>:SEQuencer:MODE
SYSTem:SEQuencer
