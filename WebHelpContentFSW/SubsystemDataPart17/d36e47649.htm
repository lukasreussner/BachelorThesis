[SENSe:]CDPower:OPERation <Mode>

The operation mode is used for the channel search.

Parameters:
<Mode>
ACCess | TRAFfic
ACCess

Only PICH (always available) and DATA channels can exist.

TRAFfic

All channels (PICH/RRI/DATA/ACK and DRC) can exist. PICH and RRI are always in the signal.

*RST:TRAFfic

For further details refer to "Operating Modes - Access and Traffic".

Example: CDP:ORD HAD

Sets Hadamard order.

TRAC? TRACE2

Reads out the results in Hadamard order.

CDP:ORD BITR

Sets BitReverse order.

TRAC? TRACE2

Reads out the results in BitReverse order.

Manual operation: See "Operation Mode"
