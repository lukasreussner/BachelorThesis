Tuning Mode

Selects the tuning or measurement mode.

For more information, see "Tuning Modes".

Note: A preamplifier can be activated or deactivated individually for different tuning modes. When you switch tuning modes, the defined preamplifier state is adapted.

"Sweep"

The measurement is based on an automatically generated frequency set.

"Frequency Table"

The measurement is based on a customized frequency table.

For more information, see "Using a Frequency Table".

"Single Frequency"

The measurement measures a single frequency only.

For more information, see "Configuring Single Frequency Measurements".

Frequency list measurement:

[SENSe:​]CONFigure:​LIST:​SINGLe

Single frequency measurement:
