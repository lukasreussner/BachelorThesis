Sample Rate

Defines or indicates the amount of data that is analyzed within the specified "Capture Time".

For DOCSIS 3.1 downstream measurements, a fixed sample rate of 204.8 MHz is used.

For DOCSIS 3.1 upstream measurements, a fixed sample rate of 102.4 MHz or 204.8 MHz (oversampling *2) can be used.

Note: Synchronous Band Power measurements require a sample rate of 204.8 MHz (see "Synchronous Band Power Settings").
