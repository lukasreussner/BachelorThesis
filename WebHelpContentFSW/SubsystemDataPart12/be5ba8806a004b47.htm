Question: Why do the EVM results for my FSK-modulated signal look wrong?

Answer:

For an FSK-modulated signal, the signal processing differs to an PSK/QAM/MSK-modulated signal. The estimation model does not minimize the EVM but the error of the instantaneous frequency (see "Error Model"). Therefore, the measurement value that corresponds to the EVM value for FSK is the Frequency Error (Absolute/Relative). (Source Type: Modulation Error; Result Type: Frequency Error (Absolute/Relative))
