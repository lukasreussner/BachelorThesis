Controlling the generator output state

The "Generator RF Output" feature turns the RF output on the connected signal generator on and off.

When you turn off the RF output, the generator does not feed a signal into the connected DUT.

Remote command: 

CONFigure:​GENerator:​RFOutput[:​STATe]

CONFigure:​GENerator:​RFOutput:​LEDState?
