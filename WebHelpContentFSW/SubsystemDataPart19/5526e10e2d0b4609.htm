SENSe:TRACking:CROSstalk <State>

Activates or deactivates the compensation for crosstalk between MIMO carriers.

This command is only available for standard IEEE 802.11ac or n (MIMO).

For details see "Crosstalk and Spectrum Flatness".

Parameters:
<State>
ON | OFF | 1 | 0
*RST:0
Example: 

SENS:TRAC:CROS ON

Manual operation: See "Compensate Crosstalk(MIMO only)"
