I/Q Analyzer > Configuration > Data Acquisition and Bandwidth Settings > Data Acquisition > Meas Time
Meas Time

Defines the I/Q acquisition time. By default, the measurement time is calculated as the number of I/Q samples ( "Record Length" ) divided by the sample rate. If you change the measurement time, the "Record Length" is automatically changed, as well.

For details on the maximum number of samples see also "Sample Rate and Maximum Usable I/Q Bandwidth for RF Input".

Remote command: 

[SENSe:​]SWEep:​TIME
