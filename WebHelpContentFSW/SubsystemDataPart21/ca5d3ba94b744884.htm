CONFigure:US:CHANnel:SBPower:CONFigure:USER:BAND<ba>:LOCation <SCarrier>

Defines the subcarrier at the specified edge of the power band to be measured (see CONFigure:​US:​CHANnel:​SBPower:​CONFigure:​USER:​BAND<ba>:​LREFerence).

Suffix: 
<ba>
1 to 20

User-configured power band

Parameters:
<SCarrier>
integer

Number of subcarrier at which the band to be measured starts or ends.

The maximum subcarrier number (<max. sc no.>) is 2*NFFT-1 (due to oversampling).

Range: 0 to <max. sc no.>
Example: CONF:US:CHAN:SBPO:CONF:USER:BAND:LREF LEFT
CONFigure:US:CHANnel:SBPower:CONFigure:USER:BAND1:LOCation 10

Defines a band to the right of subcarrier number 10.

Manual operation: See "Location [SC]"
