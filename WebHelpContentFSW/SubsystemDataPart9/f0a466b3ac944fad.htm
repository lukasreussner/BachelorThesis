Decimation

Turns decimation on and off.

When you turn on decimation, the samples that have already been used for a given half decade are resampled in lower half decades. Reusing these samples results in lower measurement times in the lower half decades, because less samples have to be recorded there.

To get valid results for lower offset frequencies, make sure to use an appropriate sample rate.

This feature is especially useful when you are measuring half decades with very low offset frequencies.

Using decimation is available for the "I/Q FFT" sweep mode.

Remote command: 

[SENSe:​]IQ:​DECimation
