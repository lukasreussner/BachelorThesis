Shift y

Shifts the y-value of each data point vertically by the defined shift width (as opposed to an additive offset defined for the entire limit line, see "Y-Offset").

Remote command: 

CALCulate<n>:​LIMit<k>:​LOWer:​SHIFt

CALCulate<n>:​LIMit<k>:​UPPer:​SHIFt
