Symbol Rate

The symbol rate also determines the I/Q bandwidth of the data recording and demodulation. You can change the default rate by entering a value in Hz.

The minimum symbol rate is 25 Hz. The maximum symbol rate depends on the defined "Sample Rate" (see "Sample Rate, Symbol Rate and I/Q Bandwidth").

Remote command: 

[SENSe:​]DDEMod:​SRATe
