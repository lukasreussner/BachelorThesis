[SENSe:]DEMod:FMVF:TYPE <Filter>

Activates or deactivates additional filters applied after demodulation to filter out unwanted signals, or correct pre-emphasized input signals.

Parameters:
<Filter>
NONE | LP01 | LP1 | LP5 | LP10 | LP25
NONE

No video filter applied

LP01

Low pass filter 0.1 % bandwidth

LP1

Low pass filter1 % bandwidth

LP5

Low pass filter 5 % bandwidth

LP10

Low pass filter 10 % bandwidth

LP25

Low pass filter 25 % bandwidth

Example: SENS:DEM:FMVF:TYPE LP01
Manual operation: See "FM Video Bandwidth"
