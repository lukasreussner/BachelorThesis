Quad Error vs Carrier

Displays the minimum, average and maximum quadrature offset (error) versus carrier in individual traces. For details on quadrature offset, see "Quadrature Offset".

Remote command: 

LAY:ADD? '1',RIGH,QUAD, see LAYout:​ADD[:​WINDow]?

Or:

CONFigure:​BURSt:​QUAD:​QCARrier[:​IMMediate]

Querying results:

TRACe[:​DATA], see "Error Vs Carrier"
