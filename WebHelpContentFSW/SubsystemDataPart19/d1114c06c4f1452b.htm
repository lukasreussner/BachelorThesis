I/Q Skew

If transmission of the data on the I path is delayed compared to the Q path, or vice versa, the I/Q data becomes skewed.

The I/Q skew results can be compensated for together with "Gain Imbalance" and "Quadrature Offset" (see "I/Q Mismatch Compensation").
