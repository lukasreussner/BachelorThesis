Signal States

Access: "Overview" > "Signal Description" > "Signal States" tab

The (nominal) frequencies or chirps the signal is expected to switch to are defined in advance in the "Signal State" table. Each possible frequency/chirp is considered to be a hop state/chirp state.These settings are only available if at least one of the additional options R&S FSW-K60C/-K60H are installed.

For details on the individual parameters see "Frequency Hopping".
