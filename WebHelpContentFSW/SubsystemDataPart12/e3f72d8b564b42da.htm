Configuration According to Digital Standards

Access: "Meas Config" > "Overview" > "Digital Standards"

Various predefined settings files for common digital standards are provided for use with the VSA application. In addition, you can create your own settings files for user-specific measurements.

For an overview of predefined standards and settings, see "Predefined Standards and Settings". For detailed instructions, see "How to Perform VSA According to Digital Standards"
