CONFigure:MEXC:SUBCarrier:COUNt <NoExclSC>

Defines the number of subcarriers to be excluded from MER calculation (for CONFigure:​MEXC:​STATeAUTO). The subcarriers with the worst MER are automatically excluded from MER calculation.

Parameters:
<NoExclSC>
integer
Range: 1  to  5
*RST:5
Example: CONF:MEXC:STAT AUTO
CONF:MEXC:SUBC:COUN 5
Manual operation: See "Excluding Subcarriers from MER Calculation"
