Spatial Mapping Mode

Defines the mapping between streams and antennas.

For details see "Spatial Mapping".

"Direct"

The mapping between streams and antennas is the identity matrix. See also section "20.3.11.10.1 Spatial Mapping" of the IEEE 802.11n WLAN standard.

"Spatial Expansion:"

For this mode all streams contribute to all antennas. See also section "20.3.11.10.1 Spatial Mapping" of the IEEE 802.11n WLAN standard.

"User defined"

The mapping between streams and antennas is defined by the "User Defined Spatial Mapping" table.

Remote command: 

CONFigure:​WLAN:​SMAPping:​MODE
