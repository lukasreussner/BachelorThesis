Unit

The unit in which the y-values of the data points of the transducer line are defined.

As soon as a transducer is activated, the unit of the transducer is automatically used for all the level settings and outputs. The unit cannot be changed in the amplitude settings unless dB is used.

For details see "Y-Axis Unit" and "Basics on Transducer Factors".

Remote command: 

[SENSe:​]CORRection:​TRANsducer:​UNIT
