Sample Rate

This is the sample rate the R&S FSW 802.11ad application expects the I/Q input data to have. For standard IEEE 802.11ad measurements, a sample rate of 2.64 MHz is used.

The R&S FSW 802.11ad application does not resample the data. To measure signals with a sample rate other than the standard 2.64 MHz for IEEE 802.11ad signals, change this setting.

Remote command: 

TRACe:​IQ:​SRATe
