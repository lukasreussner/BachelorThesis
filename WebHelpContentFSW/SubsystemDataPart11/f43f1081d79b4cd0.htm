CALCulate<n>:CHRDetection:POWer:REFerence <Reference>

Defines the reference point for positioning the power measurement range.

For details on the measurement range parameters see "Measurement Range".

Suffix: 
<n>
irrelevant
Parameters:
<Reference>
CENTer | EDGE | FMSettling | PMSettling
EDGE

The measurement range is defined in reference to the chirp's rising or falling edge (see CALCulate<n>:​CHRDetection:​FREQuency:​OFFSet:​BEGin and CALCulate<n>:​CHRDetection:​FREQuency:​OFFSet:​END).

CENTer

The measurement range is defined in reference to the center of the chirp.

FMSettling

The measurement range starts at the FM settling point (see [SENSe:​]CHIRp:​FREQuency:​FMSPoint?).

PMSettling

The measurement range starts at the PM settling point (see [SENSe:​]CHIRp:​FREQuency:​PMSPoint?).

Example: CALC:CHRD:POW:REF EDGE
