Configuration

Access: [MODE] > "802.11ad"

IEEE 802.11ad measurements require a special application on the R&S FSW.

The default IEEE 802.11ad Modulation Accuracy measurement captures the I/Q data from the IEEE 802.11ad Modulation Accuracy measurement signal and determines various characteristic signal parameters such as the modulation accuracy, channel frequency response, and power gain in just one measurement (see "IEEE 802.11ad Modulation Accuracy Measurement")

Other parameters specified in the IEEE 802.11ad standard must be determined in separate measurements (see "SEM Measurements").

The settings required to configure each of these measurements are described here.
