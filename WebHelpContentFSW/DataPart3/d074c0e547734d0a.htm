Amplitude Settings

Access: "Overview" > "Amplitude"

Amplitude settings determine how the R&S FSW must process or display the expected input power levels.

The scaling settings are described in "Scaling the Y-Axis".

The remote commands required to define these settings are described in "Amplitude Settings".
