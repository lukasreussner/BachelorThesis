CONFigure:CDPower[:BTS]:CTABle:RESTore

This command restores the predefined channel tables to their factory-set values. In this way, you can undo unintentional overwriting.

Example: CONF:CDP:CTAB:REST

Restores the channel table.

Usage: Event
Manual operation: See "Restoring Default Tables"
