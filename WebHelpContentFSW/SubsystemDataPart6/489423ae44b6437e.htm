[SENSe:]FREQuency:CENTer:STEP:LINK:FACTor <Factor>

This command defines a step size factor if the center frequency step size is coupled to the span.

Parameters:
<Factor>
1 to 100 PCT
*RST:10
Example: 

//Couple frequency step size to span and define a step size factor

FREQ:CENT:STEP:LINK SPAN
FREQ:CENT:STEP:LINK:FACT 20PCT
