TRIGger[:SEQuence]:SLOPe <Type>
Parameters:
<Type>
POSitive | NEGative
POSitive

Triggers when the signal rises to the trigger level (rising edge).

NEGative

Triggers when the signal drops to the trigger level (falling edge).

*RST:POSitive
Example: TRIG:SLOP NEG
