[SENSe:]IQ:FFT:ALGorithm <Method>

Defines the FFT calculation method.

For more information see Basics on FFT.

Parameters:
<Method>
SINGle

One FFT is calculated for the entire record length; if the FFT length is larger than the record length (see [SENSe:​]IQ:​FFT:​LENGth and TRACe:​IQ:​RLENgth), zeros are appended to the captured data.

AVERage

Several overlapping FFTs are calculated for each record; the results are averaged to determine the final FFT result for the record.

The user-defined window length and window overlap are used (see [SENSe:​]IQ:​FFT:​WINDow:​LENGth and [SENSe:​]IQ:​FFT:​WINDow:​OVERlap).

*RST:AVER
Example: IQ:FFT:ALG SING
Manual operation: See "Transformation Algorithm"
