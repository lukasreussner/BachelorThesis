[SENSe:]CONFigure:FREQuency:SINGle

This command configures the software to perform a single frequency measurement in single sweep mode.

Example: FREQ:SING 20MHz

Defines a measurement frequency of 20 MHz.

CONF:FREQ:SING

INIT

Selects and initiates a single frequency measurement.

Usage: Event
Manual operation: See "Tuning Mode"
