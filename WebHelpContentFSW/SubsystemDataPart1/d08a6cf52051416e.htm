B2000/B5000 State

Activates the optional 2 GHz / 5 GHz bandwidth extension (R&S FSW-B2000/B5000).

Note: Manual operation on the connected oscilloscope, or remote operation other than by the R&S FSW, is not possible while the B2000/B5000 option is active.
When the B2000/B5000 option is activated, the basic [IF OUT 2 GHz/ IF OUT 5 GHz] output is automatically deactivated. It is not reactivated when the B2000/B5000 option is switched off.

For details on prerequisites and restrictions, see "Basics on the 2 GHz / 5 GHz Bandwidth Extensions (R&S FSW-B2000/B5000 Options)".

Remote command: 

SYSTem:​COMMunicate:​RDEVice:​OSCilloscope[:​STATe]
