[SENSe:]POWer:ACHannel:SSETup <State>

This command defines whether adjacent channels are defined symmetrically or not.

For more information see "Measurement on Multi-Standard Radio (MSR) Signals".

Parameters:
<State>
ON | OFF | 1 | 0
ON | 1

The upper and lower adjacent and alternate channels are defined symmetrically. This is the default behaviour and corresponds to the behavior in firmware versions before 2.10.

OFF | 0

The upper and lower channels can be configured differently.

*RST:1
Manual operation: See "Symmetrical Adjacent Setup"
