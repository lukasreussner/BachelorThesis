[SENSe:][LTE:]DL:TRACking:TIME <State>

This command turns timing tracking on and off.

Parameters:
<State>
ON | OFF | 1 | 0
*RST:OFF
Example: 

//Turn on timing tracking

DL:TRAC:TIME ON
Manual operation: See "Timing"
