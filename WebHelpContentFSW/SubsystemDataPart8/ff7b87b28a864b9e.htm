DISPlay[:WINDow<n>]:TRACe:SYMBols <State>

This command turns symbols that represent the measurement points on a trace on and off.

Suffix: 
<n>

Window

Parameters:
<State>
ON | OFF | 1 | 0
*RST:0
Example: DISP:WIND2:TRAC:SYMB ON

Switches on the display of symbols in window 2..

Manual operation: See "Symbols"
