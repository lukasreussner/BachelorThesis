Automatic Settings

Access: [AUTO SET]

Some settings can be adjusted by the R&S FSW automatically according to the current measurement settings. In order to do so, a measurement is performed. The duration of this measurement can be defined automatically or manually.

MSRA operating mode

In MSRA operating mode, the following automatic settings are not available, as they require a new data acquisition. However, 3GPP FDD applications cannot perform data acquisition in MSRA operating mode.
