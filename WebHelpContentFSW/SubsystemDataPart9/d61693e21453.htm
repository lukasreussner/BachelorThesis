X-Axis Start / Stop

Defines the start and stop frequency of the horizontal axis.

Note that the displayed frequency range is a detail of the measurement range. Regardless of the displayed frequency range, the application still performs all measurement over the measurement range you have defined.

The range depends on the measurement range. and possible increments correspond to the half decades.

Available for a manual "X Axis Scope".

Remote command: 

X-axis start:

DISPlay[:​WINDow]:​TRACe:​X[:​SCALe]:​STARt

X-axis stop:

DISPlay[:​WINDow]:​TRACe:​X[:​SCALe]:​STOP
