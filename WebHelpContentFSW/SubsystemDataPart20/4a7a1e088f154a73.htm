802.11ad measurements application (R&S FSW-K95) > Remote Commands for IEEE 802.11ad Measurements > Configuring the IEEE 802.11ad Modulation Accuracy Measurement > Signal Capturing > General Capture Settings > TRACe:IQ:SRATe
TRACe:IQ:SRATe <SampleRate>
Parameters:
<SampleRate>

For standard IEEE 802.11ad signals, a sample rate of 2.64 GHz is used (requires an optional bandwidth extension with at least 2 GHz).

The valid sample rates are described in Sample Rate and Maximum Usable I/Q Bandwidth for RF Input.

Default unit: HZ
Manual operation: See "Sample Rate"
