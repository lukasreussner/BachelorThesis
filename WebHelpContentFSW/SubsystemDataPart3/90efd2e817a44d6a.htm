OUTPut:ADEMod[:ONLine]:PHONes <State>

In addition to sending the output to the IF/VIDEO/DEMOD output connector (on the rear panel of the R&S FSW), it can also be output to headphones connected on the front panel ([Phones] connector).

CAUTION: To protect your hearing, make sure that the volume setting is not too high before putting on the headphones.

If you do not hear output on the connected headphones despite having enabled both general online demod output OUTPut:​ADEMod[:​ONLine][:​STATe] and this command, adjust the volume setting.

(Using SYSTem:​SPEaker:​VOLume.

Parameters:
<State>
ON | OFF | 1 | 0
*RST:0
Example: OUTP:ADEM:PHON ON
Manual operation: See "Phones Output"
