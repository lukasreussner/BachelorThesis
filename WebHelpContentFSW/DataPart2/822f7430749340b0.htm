Signal Count Marker State

Activates or deactivates the special signal count marker function.

When activated, the sweep stops at the reference marker until the signal counter has delivered a result.

For details see "Precise Frequency (Signal Count) Marker".

Remote command: 

CALCulate<n>:​MARKer<m>:​COUNt

CALCulate<n>:​MARKer<m>:​COUNt:​FREQuency?
