Selecting the unit for phase results

You can display phase information either in degrees or radians. Select the preferred unit from the "Unit" dropdown menu.

The selected unit applies to the AM/PM results and the Phase Deviation vs Time result.

Remote command: 

CALCulate<n>:​UNIT:​ANGLe
