DISPlay[:WINDow<n>]:TDOMain:Y[:SCALe]:NORMalise[:STATe] <State>

This command turns normalization of the results in the Time Domain result display on and off.

Suffix: 
<n>

Window

Parameters:
<State>
ON | OFF | 1 | 0
Example: DISP:TDOM:Y:NORM ON

Normalizes the results in the Time Domain result display to 1.

Manual operation: See "Configuring the Time Domain result display"
