Mainlobe Power (Average)

Peak power of the correlator output, normalized to the reference waveform power and to the correlation interval. For perfectly correlated measured and reference waveforms, this value corresponds to the average power of the measured waveform over the correlation interval.

For details see "Mainlobe power (integrated)".
