DISPlay[:WINDow<n>]:TRACe<t>:X[:SCALe]:AUTO <State>

This command turns automatic scaling of the x-axis in graphical result displays on and off.

Suffix: 
<n>

Window

<t>

irrelevant

Parameters:
<State>
OFF

Selects manual scaling of the diagram.

ON

Automatically scales the diagram when new results are available.

ONCE

Automatically scales the diagram once whenever required.

Example: DISP:TRAC:X:AUTO ON

Scales the axis each time new results are available.

Manual operation: See "Scaling the x-axis automatically"
