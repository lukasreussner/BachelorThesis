Generator IP Address

Defines the IP address of the signal generator connected to the analyzer via LAN.

If you are not sure about the IP address of your generator, kindly ask your IT administrator if they can provide one.

Remote command: 

CONFigure:​GENerator:​IPConnection:​ADDRess

CONFigure:​GENerator:​IPConnection:​LEDState?
