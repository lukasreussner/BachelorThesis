Optimizing and Troubleshooting

If the results do not meet your expectations, or if problems occur during measurement, try the following solutions.

└ Too many hop/chirp states have been detected in auto mode
└ The desired hop/chirp states are not detected
└ Instead of one hop/chirp, several shorter hop/chirps of the same hop/chirp state are detected
└ Instead of one hop/chirp, several shorter hop/chirps of a different hop/chirp state are detected
└ One or more shorter hops/chirps are detected directly before or after the desired hop/chirp
└ Spectrogram of a selected hop/chirp is empty
