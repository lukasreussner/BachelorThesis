IF OUT 2 GHz Connector

The female SMA connector is only available for instrument models R&S FSW26/43/50/67/85. It can be used to provide intermediate frequency (IF) output of approximately 2 GHz at a frequency of 2 GHz.

Output is activated in the software (INPUT/OUTPUT key).

For details, see "IF and Video Signal Output".
