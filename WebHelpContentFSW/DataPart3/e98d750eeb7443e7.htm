Harmonic RBW Auto

Enables/disables the automatic adjustment of the resolution bandwidth for Normal (3dB) (Gaussian) and 5-Pole filter types.

The automatic adjustment is carried out according to:

"RBWn = RBW1 * n"

If RBWn is not available, the next higher value is used.

Remote command: 

CALCulate<n>:​MARKer<m>:​FUNCtion:​HARMonics:​BANDwidth:​AUTO
