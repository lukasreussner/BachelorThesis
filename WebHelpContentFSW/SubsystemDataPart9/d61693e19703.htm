Digital PLL

Turns an additional frequency correction based on the I/Q data on and off.

If on, the application is able to track frequency changes during the I/Q data capture that would otherwise fall into the half decade measurement bandwidth (see Max Freq Drift).

The digital PLL works for all half decades measured in I/Q mode.

Remote command: 

[SENSe:​]IQ:​DPLL
