WLAN application (R&S FSW-K91) > Remote Commands for WLAN 802.11 Measurements > Configuring the WLAN IQ Measurement (Modulation Accuracy, Flatness and Tolerance) > Tracking and Channel Estimation > SENSe:TRACking:PHASe
SENSe:TRACking:PHASe <State>

Activates or deactivates the compensation for phase drifts. If activated, the measurement results are compensated for phase drifts on a per-symbol basis.

For details see "Tracking the phase drift, timing jitter and gain".

Parameters:
<State>
ON | OFF | 0 | 1
*RST:1
Example: SENS:TRAC:PHAS ON
Manual operation: See "Phase Tracking"
