[SENSe:]SWEep:EGATe:TRACe<t>[:STATe<range>] <State>

This command includes or excludes a gate range for a particular trace.

Suffix: 
<t>

Trace

<range>
1...3

gate range

Parameters:
<State>
ON | OFF | 1 | 0
*RST:0
Example: SWE:EGAT:TRAC1:STAT1 ON

Activates gate range 1 for trace 1.
