Problem: The spectrum is not displayed in the logarithmic domain
Solution:
  • 1. Select the measurement window.
  • 2. Press the [AMPT] key.
  • 3. Press the "Unit" softkey.
  • 4. Press the "Y-Axis Unit" softkey.
  • 5. Select dB.
