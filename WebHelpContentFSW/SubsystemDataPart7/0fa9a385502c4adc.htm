Amplifier Measurements (R&S FSW-K18) > Remote Control Commands for Amplifier Measurements > Configuring Amplifier Measurements > Applying Digital Predistortion > CONFigure:DDPD:COUNt
CONFigure:DDPD:COUNt <Count>

This command defines the number of iterations in a direct DPD sequence.

  • Turn on direct DPD (CONFigure:DDPD[:STATe]).
Parameters:
<Count>
<numeric value> (integer only)
Range: 1  to  1000
*RST:10
Example: 

//Define number of iterations

CONF:DDPD:COUN 25
Manual operation: See "Running a direct DPD sequence"
