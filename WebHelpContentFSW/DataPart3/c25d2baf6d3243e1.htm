CALCulate<n>:FLINe<k>:STATe <State>

This command turns a frequency line on and off

Suffix: 
<n>

Window

<k>

Limit line

Parameters:
<State>
ON | OFF | 1 | 0
*RST:0
Example: CALC:FLIN2:STAT ON

Turns frequency line 2 on.
