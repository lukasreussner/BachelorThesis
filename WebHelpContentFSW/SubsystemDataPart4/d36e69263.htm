READ:SPECtrum:WMODulation:GATing?

This command reads out the gating settings for gated Wide Modulation Spectrum measurements. It is identical to READ:​SPECtrum:​WMODulation:​GATing? and is maintained for compatibility reasons only.

Example: READ:SPEC:WMOD:GAT?
Usage: Query only
Mode: GSM
