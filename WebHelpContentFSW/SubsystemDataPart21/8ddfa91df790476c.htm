Minislot Pilot Pattern

Defines the pilot pattern used by the specified minislots. Which patterns are available depends on the "NFFT (FFT length)".

  • 2K mode: 1 to 7
  • 4K mode: 8 to 14

For more information, see "Pilot patterns".

Remote command: 

CONFigure:​US:​CHANnel:​PCONfig<pri>:​MINislot:​PPATtern
