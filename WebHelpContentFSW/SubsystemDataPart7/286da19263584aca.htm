Attach to R&S FSW Frequency

Turns synchronization of the analyzer and generator frequency on and off.

When you turn on this feature, changing the frequency on the analyzer automatically adjusts the frequency on the generator.

Remote command: 

CONFigure:​GENerator:​FREQuency:​CENTer:​SYNC[:​STATe]
