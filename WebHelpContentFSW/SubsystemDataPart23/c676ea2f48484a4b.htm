CONFigure[:LTE]:UL[:CC<cc>]:PRACh:FRINdex <FRINdex>

This command selects the PRACH frequency index.

Suffix: 
<cc>

Component Carrier

Parameters:
<FRINdex>
<numeric value>
Example: CONF:UL:PRAC:FRIN 10

Selects the frequency index 10.

Manual operation: See "PRACH Preamble Mapping"
