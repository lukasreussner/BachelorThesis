X-Axis

Describes the horizontal axis on which the data points of the limit line are defined. Includes the following settings:

  • Unit:
    –"Hz": for frequency domain
    –"s": for time domain
  • Scaling mode: absolute or relative values
    For relative values, the frequencies are referred to the currently set center frequency. In the time domain, the left boundary of the diagram is used as the reference.
  • Scaling: linear or logarithmic
