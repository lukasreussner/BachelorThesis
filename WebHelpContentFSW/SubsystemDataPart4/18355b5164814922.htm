Frequency Band

The frequency band defines the frequency range used to transmit the signal.

For details see "Frequency bands and channels".

The following frequency bands are supported:

  • DCS 1800
  • E-GSM 900
  • GSM 450
  • GSM 480
  • GSM 710
  • GSM 750
  • GSM 850
  • PCS 1900
  • P-GSM 900
  • R-GSM 900
  • T-GSM 380
  • T-GSM 410
  • T-GSM 810
  • T-GSM 900

The default frequency band is "E-GSM 900".

Remote command: 

CONFigure[:​MS]:​NETWork[:​TYPE]

CONFigure[:​MS]:​NETWork:​FREQuency:​BAND
