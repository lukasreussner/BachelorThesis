CONFigure:WSPectrum:MODulation:LIST:SELect <Mode>

For "Modulation Spectrum Table" measurements, this command controls whether offset frequencies are measured up to 1800 kHz or 5800 kHz.

Parameters for setting and query: 
<Mode>
NARRow

The frequency list comprises offset frequencies up to 1.8 MHz from the carrier. The sample rate is 6.5 MHz.

NSParse

More compact version of "NARRow". The sample rate is 6.5 MHz.

WIDE

The frequency list comprises offset frequencies up to 6 MHz from the carrier. The sample rate is 19.5 MHz.

WSParse

More compact version of WIDE. The sample rate is 19.5 MHz.

*RST:WIDE
Example: CONFigure:WSPectrum:MODulation:LIST:SELect NARRow
Manual operation: See "Modulation Spectrum Table: Frequency List"

For a detailed example see "Programming Example: Determining the EVM".
