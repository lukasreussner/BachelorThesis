LTE Downlink (R&S FSW-K10x) > Remote Control > Analysis > Result Settings > UNIT:CAXes
UNIT:CAXes <Unit>

This command selects the scale of the x-axis for result displays that show subcarrier results.

Parameters:
<Unit>
CARR

Shows the number of the subcarriers on the x-axis.

HZ

Shows the frequency of the subcarriers on the x-axis.

Example: 

//Display frequency on the x-axis

UNIT:CAX HZ
Manual operation: See "Carrier Axes"
