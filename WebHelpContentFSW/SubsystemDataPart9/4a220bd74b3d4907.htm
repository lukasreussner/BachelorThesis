Copy Trace

Access: "Overview" > "Analysis" > "Traces" > "Copy Trace"

Or : [TRACE] > "Copy Trace"

Copies trace data to another trace.

The first group of buttons (labeled "Trace 1" to "Trace 6" ) selects the source trace. The second group of buttons (labeled "Copy to Trace 1" to "Copy to Tace 6" ) selects the destination.

Remote command: 

TRACe<n>:​COPY
