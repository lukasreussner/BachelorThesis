CONFigure:DS:CHANnel:TIDepth <TimeInterDepth>

Defines the maximum number of delay lines used for time interleaving. The required depth depends on the symbol duration, that is the subcarrier spacing.

Setting parameters: 
<TimeInterDepth>
Range: 1  to  16 (for NFFT = 8K mode); 32 (for NFFT = 4K mode)
*RST:16 (NFFT = 4K mode)
Example: CONF:DS:CHAN:TID 16
