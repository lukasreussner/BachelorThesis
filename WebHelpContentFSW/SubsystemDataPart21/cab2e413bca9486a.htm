CONFigure:DS:CHANnel:FCONfig<cwi>:PROFile <ProfileType>

Assigns one of the active profiles defined in the "Profile List" to the selected codeword.

To determine whether a profile is active or not, use the CONFigure:​DS:​CHANnel:​PCONfig<pri>:​STATe? query.

Suffix: 
<cwi>
1..n

codeword index in the "Codeword / Frame Configuration" table

Parameters:
<ProfileType>
A | B | C | D | E | F | G | H | I | J | K | L | M | N | O | P | NONE
Example: CONF:DS:CHAN:FCON2:PROF A
Example: 

For a detailed example see "Measurement 1: Measuring Modulation Accuracy".

Manual operation: See "Profile"
