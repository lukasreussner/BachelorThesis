Enable Parallel BB Capture

Turns simultaneous data capture on the RF input and the analog baseband input on and off.

This is necessary when you perform measurements that take into account the supply voltage and the current drawn by the PA.

Remote command: 

INPut:​SELect:​BBANalog[:​STATe]
