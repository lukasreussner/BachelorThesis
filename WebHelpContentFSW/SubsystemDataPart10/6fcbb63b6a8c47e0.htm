CALCulate<n>:MARKer<m>:X:SLIMits[:STATe] <State>

This command turns marker search limits on and off for all markers in all windows.

Suffix: 
<n>, <m>

irrelevant

Parameters:
<State>
ON | OFF | 1 | 0
*RST:0
Example: CALC:MARK:X:SLIM ON

Switches on search limitation.
