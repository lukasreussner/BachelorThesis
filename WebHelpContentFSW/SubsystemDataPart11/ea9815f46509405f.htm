Defines the measurement bandwidth in Hz.

Note that the sample rate and the measurement bandwidth are interdependent (see [SENSe:​]SRATe). For information on supported sample rates and bandwidths see the data sheet.

Parameters:
<Bandwidth>
Range: 80 Hz  to  depends on options installed
*RST:maximum allowed
Default unit: HZ
