Power vs Time

Access: "Overview" > "Measurement" > "Power vs Time"

The "Power vs Time" filter is used to suppress out-of-band interference in the Power vs Time measurement (see "Power vs Time Filter"). A limit line is available to determine if the power exceeds the limits defined by the standard in each slot.
