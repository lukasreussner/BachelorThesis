Auto Fix Range

This command defines the use of fixed value limits.

"None"

Both the upper and lower limits are determined by automatic scaling of the y-axis.

"Lower"

The lower limit is fixed (defined by the "Minimum / Maximum" settings), while the upper limit is determined by automatic scaling of the y-axis.

"Upper"

The upper limit is fixed (defined by the "Minimum / Maximum" settings), while the lower limit is determined by automatic scaling of the y-axis.

Remote command: 

DISPlay[:​WINDow<n>]:​TRACe<t>:​Y[:​SCALe]:​AUTO:​FIXed:​RANGe
