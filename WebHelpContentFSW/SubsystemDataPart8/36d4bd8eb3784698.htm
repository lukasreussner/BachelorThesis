Preamplifier

Turns the preamplifier on and off.

Turn on the preamplifier for a low inherent noise of the analyzer.

For R&S FSW26 or higher models, the gain is 30 dB if the preamplifier is activated.

For the other models, you can select a gain of 15 dB or 30 dB.

Remote command: 

INPut<ip>:​GAIN:​STATe
