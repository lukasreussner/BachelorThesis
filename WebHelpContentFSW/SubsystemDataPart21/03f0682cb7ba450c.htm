Retrieving Trace Results

The following commands describe how to retrieve the trace data from the DOCSIS 3.1 I/Q measurement (Modulation Accuracy).

The traces for frequency sweep measurements are identical to those in the Spectrum application.

List of Commands
FORMat[:DATA]
TRACe<t>[:DATA]?
TRACe<n>[:DATA]:X?
