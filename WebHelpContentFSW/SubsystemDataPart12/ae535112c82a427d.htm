Amplitude Settings

Access: [AMPT] > "Amplitude Config"

Amplitude settings affect the signal power or error levels.

The scaling settings are described in "Scaling".

Amplitude settings for analog baseband input

Amplitude settings for analog baseband input are described in Amplitude Settings for Analog Baseband Input.

Note that amplitude settings are not window-specific, as opposed to the scaling and unit settings.
