Automatic Settings

Access: [AUTO SET] menu

Some settings can be adjusted by the R&S FSW automatically according to the current measurement settings and signal characteristics.

MSRA operating mode

In MSRA operating mode, the following automatic settings are not available, as they require a new data acquisition. However, the R&S FSW WLAN application cannot perform data acquisition in MSRA operating mode.
