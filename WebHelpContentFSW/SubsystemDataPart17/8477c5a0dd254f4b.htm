CONFigure:CDPower[:BTS]:CTABle[:STATe] <State>

This command switches the channel table on or off.

Parameters:
<State>
ON | OFF | 1 | 0
*RST:0
Example: 

CONF:CDP:CTAB ON

Manual operation: See "Using Predefined Channel Tables"
