DISPlay[:WINDow<n>][:SUBWindow<w>]:COUPling <State>

This command couples or decouples result display tabs (subwindows).

Subwindow coupling is available for measurements with multiple data streams (like carrier aggregation).

Suffix: 
<n>

Window

<w>

Subwindow

Parameters:
<State>
ON | OFF | 1 | 0
*RST:OFF
Example: 

//Turn on subwindow coupling

DISP:COUP ON
Manual operation: See "Subwindow Coupling"
