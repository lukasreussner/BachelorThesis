Scale of the y-axis (display settings for the Time Domain)

The scale of the y-axis also depends on your configuration.

The signal characteristics displayed in the Time Domain result display all have a different unit. Therefore, the application provides a feature that normalizes all results to 1 (see "Configuring the Time Domain result display"). Normalization makes it easier to compare the timing between the traces. By default, normalization is on. Note that you can normalize each Time Domain window individually.

Unnormalized results are displayed in their respective unit.
