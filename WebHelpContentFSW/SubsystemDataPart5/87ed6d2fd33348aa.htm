Retrieving Marker Results

The following commands are used to retrieve the results of markers.

List of Commands
CALCulate<n>:DELTamarker<m>:X:RELative?
CALCulate<n>:DELTamarker<m>:Y?
CALCulate<n>:MARKer<m>:Y?
