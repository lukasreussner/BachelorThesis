Delete Range

Deletes the currently focused range, if possible. (The reference range cannot be deleted. A minimum of three ranges is required.) The range numbers are updated accordingly.

For more details, see "Ranges and Range Settings".

Remote command: 

[SENSe:​]ESPectrum<sb>:​RANGe<range>:​DELete
