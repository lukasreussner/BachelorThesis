802.11ad measurements application (R&S FSW-K95) > Remote Commands for IEEE 802.11ad Measurements > Configuring the IEEE 802.11ad Modulation Accuracy Measurement > Tracking > SENSe:TRACking:IQMComp
SENSe:TRACking:IQMComp <State>

Activates or deactivates the compensation for I/Q mismatch (gain imbalance, quadrature offset, I/Q skew, see "Modulation Accuracy Parameters").

Parameters:
<State>
ON | OFF | 1 | 0
ON | 1

Compensation for gain imbalance, quadrature offset, and I/Q skew impairments is applied.

OFF | 0

Compensation is not applied; this setting is required for measurements strictly according to the IEEE 802.11ad standard

*RST:0
Manual operation: See "I/Q Mismatch Compensation"
