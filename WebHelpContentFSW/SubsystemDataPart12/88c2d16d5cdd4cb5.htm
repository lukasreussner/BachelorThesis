Pattern Search

Access: "Overview" > "Burst/Pattern" > "Pattern Search"

or: [MEAS CONFIG] > "Burst/Pattern Search" > "Pattern Search" tab

The "Pattern Search" settings define when a pattern is detected in the evaluated signal.

A live preview of the capture buffer with the current settings is displayed in the preview area at the bottom of the dialog box. The blue bars indicate the detected pattern waveform (possibly even if the pattern symbols are not all correct). The green bar indicates the result range. The preview area is not editable directly.

For details on patterns see "Overview of the Demodulation Process".
