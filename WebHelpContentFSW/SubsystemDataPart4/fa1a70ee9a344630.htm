Programming Example: Measuring Statistics

This example demonstrates how to determine statistical values for a measurement in a remote environment.

-----------Configuring the measurement ------------
*RST
//Reset the instrument
CALC:MARK:FUNC:POW:SEL OBW
//Activate occupied bandwidth measurement.


--------------Performing the Measurement-----
INIT:CONT OFF
//Selects single sweep mode.
INIT;*WAI
//Initiates a new measurement and waits until the sweep has finished.


---------------Retrieving Results-------------
CALC:MARK:FUNC:POW:RES? OBW
//Returns the results for the OBW measurement.
