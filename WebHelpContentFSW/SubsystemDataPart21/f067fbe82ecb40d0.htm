Codewords (downstream only)

Determines whether codewords are decoded or not.

If the codewords are not decoded, calculation time decreases; however, in this case codeword error bits are not evaluated (in the "Signal Content Detailed" display).

Remote command: 

[SENSe:​]DEMod:​DECode:​CODewords
