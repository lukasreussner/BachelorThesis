Display Marker Numbers

By default, the marker numbers are indicated in the diagram so you can find the peaks from the list. However, for large numbers of peaks the marker numbers may decrease readability; in this case, deactivate the marker number display.

Remote command: 

CALCulate<n>:​MARKer<m>:​FUNCtion:​FPEaks:​ANNotation:​LABel[:​STATe]
