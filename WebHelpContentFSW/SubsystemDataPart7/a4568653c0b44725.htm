[SENSe:]SWEep:IQAVg:MAVerage[:STATe] <State>

This command turns moving average on and off.

  • Turn on I/Q averaging ([SENSe:]SWEep:IQAVg[:STATe]).
Parameters:
<State>
ON | OFF | 1 | 0
*RST:0
Example: 

//Turn on moving average

SWE:IQAV ON
SWE:IQAV:MAV ON
Manual operation: See "Averaging the I/Q data"
