Compressed Mode

If compressed mode is switched on, some slots of a channel are suppressed. To keep the overall data rate, the slots just before or just behind a compressed gap can be sent with half spreading factor (SF/2). This mode must be enabled to detect compressed mode channels (see "BTS Channel Types").

Remote command: 

[SENSe:​]CDPower:​PCONtrol
