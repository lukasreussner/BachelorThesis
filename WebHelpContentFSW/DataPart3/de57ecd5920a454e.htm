Adding or Removing a Power Class

Adds a new power class at the end of the list or removes the last power class. After adding or removing, the last power class is adapted to end at "+INF". Note that a maximum of four power classes are available.

Remote command: 

CALCulate<n>:​LIMit<k>:​ESPectrum<sb>:​PCLass:​COUNt
