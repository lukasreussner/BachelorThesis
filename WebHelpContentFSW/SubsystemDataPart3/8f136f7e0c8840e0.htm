Span

Defines the frequency span. The center frequency is kept constant. The following range is allowed:

span = 0: 0 Hz

span >0:

spanmin ≤ f span ≤ f max

and fmax=DBW/2

fmax and spanmin are specified in the data sheet.

For more information see Defining the Scope of the Measurement - Frequency Range.

Remote command: 

[SENSe:​]ADEMod<n>:​SPECtrum:​SPAN[:​MAXimum]

[SENSe:​]ADEMod<n>:​SPECtrum:​SPAN:​ZOOM
