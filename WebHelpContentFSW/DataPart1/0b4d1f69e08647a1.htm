[SENSe:]BWIDth:VIDeo:TYPE <Mode>
[SENSe:]BANDwidth:VIDeo:TYPE <Mode>

This command selects the position of the video filter in the signal path.

Parameters:
<Mode>
LINear

The video filter is applied in front of the logarithmic amplifier.

In linear mode, measurements with a logarithmic level scale result in flatter falling edges compared to logarithmic mode. The reason is the conversion of linear power values into logarithmic level values: if you halve the linear power, the logarithmic level decreases by 3 dB.

LOGarithmic

The video filter is applied after the logarithmic amplifier

*RST:LOGarithmic
Example: BAND:VID:TYPE LIN

Video filter ahead of the logarithmic amplifier
