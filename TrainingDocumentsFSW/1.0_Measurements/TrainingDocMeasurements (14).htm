Zero Span

A sweep in the time domain at the specified (center) frequency, i.e. the frequency span is set to zero. The display shows the time on the x-axis and the signal level on the y-axis, as on an oscilloscope. On the time axis, the grid lines correspond to 1/10 of the current sweep time.

Use the general measurement settings to configure the measurement, e.g. via the "Overview" (see "Common Measurement Settings").

Most result evaluations can also be used for zero span measurements, although some functions (e.g. markers) may work slightly differently and some may not be available. If so, this will be indicated in the function descriptions (see "Common Analysis and Display Functions").

Remote command: 

[SENSe:​]FREQuency:​SPAN

INITiate<n>[:​IMMediate]

INITiate<n>:​CONTinuous
