CALCulate<n>:LIMit<k>:ACPower:ACHannel:ABSolute:STATe <State>,<State>

This command turns the absolute limit check for the adjacent channels on and off.

You have to activate the general ACLR limit check before using this command with CALCulate<n>:​LIMit<k>:​ACPower[:​STATe].

Suffix: 
<n>, <k>

irrelevant

Parameters:
<State>
ON | OFF | 1 | 0

Absolute limit check for lower adjacent channel

*RST:0
<State>
ON | OFF | 1 | 0

Absolute limit check for upper adjacent channel

*RST:0
