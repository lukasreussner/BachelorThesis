Starting a Measurement

When a WLAN measurement channel is activated on the R&S FSW, a WLAN IQ measurement (Modulation Accuracy, Flatness and Tolerance, see "WLAN I/Q Measurement (Modulation Accuracy, Flatness and Tolerance)"), is started immediately. However, you can stop and start a new measurement any time.

Furthermore, you can perform a sequence of measurements using the Sequencer (see "Multiple Measurement Channels and Sequencer Function").
