Description of the VOR/ILS Measurement Demodulator

The following chapter describes the functions of the VOR/ILS measurement demodulator in the R&S FSW Avionics (VOR/ILS) measurements application.

By sampling (digitization) already at the IF and digital down-conversion to the baseband (I/Q), the demodulator achieves maximum accuracy and temperature stability. There is no evidence of typical errors of an analog down-conversion and demodulation like AM ⇔ FM conversion, deviation error, frequency response or frequency drift at DC coupling.
