Frequency Coupling

Defines frequency correction characteristics.

The generator frequency is calculated as follows.

With:
  • fGenerator= generator frequency
  • fLO= frequency of the analyzer's LO
  • foffset<1>= offset frequency of the analyzer
  • foffset<2>= offset frequency of other components

Use the factors and offsets to compensate one or more components in the test setup which change the frequency between the generator and the DUT. Make sure, however, that the result does not exceed the frequency range of the generator.

The application calculates the resulting start and stop frequency based on the values you apply and displays them below the equation.

Remote command: 

SOURce:​EXTernal:​FREQuency[:​FACTor]:​DENominator

SOURce:​EXTernal:​FREQuency[:​FACTor]:​NUMerator

SOURce:​EXTernal:​FREQuency:​OFFSet<offset>
