Sample Rate

Defines the number of samples to capture per symbol. The sample rate in MHz is indicated for reference. This parameter affects the demodulation bandwidth and thus the usable I/Q bandwidth.

The maximum sample rate depends on the defined "Symbol Rate" (see "Sample Rate, Symbol Rate and I/Q Bandwidth").

For details on selecting the suitable sample rate, see "Sample Rate, Symbol Rate and I/Q Bandwidth".

Remote command: 

[SENSe:​]DDEMod:​PRATe
