Center Frequency

Defines the frequency of the signal that the generator transmits.

When you turn on Attach to R&S FSW Frequency, any changes you make to the generator frequency are also adjusted on the analyzer.

Remote command: 

CONFigure:​GENerator:​FREQuency:​CENTer

CONFigure:​GENerator:​FREQuency:​CENTer:​LEDState?
