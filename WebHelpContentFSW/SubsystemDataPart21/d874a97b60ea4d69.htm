CONFigure:US:CHANnel:SBPower:CONFigure:USER:BAND<ba>[:STATe] <BandState>

This command specifies whether the user configured band will be calculated as part of the Spectrum Band Power measurement results.

Suffix: 
<ba>
1 to 20

User-configured power band

Parameters:
<BandState>
ON | OFF | 0 | 1
OFF | 0

Switches the band off

ON | 1

Switches the band on

*RST:0
Example: CONFigure:US:CHANnel:SBPower:CONFigure:USER:BAND1:STATe ON
Manual operation: See "State"
See "All Bands Off"
