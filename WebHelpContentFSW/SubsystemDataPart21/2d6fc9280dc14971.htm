Filter Out Adjacent Channels

If activated, only the useful signal is analyzed, signal data in adjacent channels is filtered out as much as possible.

This setting improves the signal to noise ratio and thus the MER results for signals with strong or a large number of adjacent channels. In particular, the filter is required for MER tests according to the DOCSIS 3.1 Physical Layer Acceptance Test Plan (see [3]).

However, for some measurements, information on the effects of adjacent channels on the measured signal may be of interest.
