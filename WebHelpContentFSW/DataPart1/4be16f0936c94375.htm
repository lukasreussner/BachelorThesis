[SENSe:]POWer:ACHannel:SPACing:GAP<gap> <Spacing>

This command defines the distance from sub block to the specified gap channel.

The channels in the upper gap are identical to those in the lower gap. Thus, in the R&S FSW MSR ACLR measurement, only 2 gap channels are configured.

The spacing for gap channels is defined in relation to the outer edges of the surrounding sub blocks, i.e.

Spacing = [CF of the gap channel] - [left sub block center] + ([RF bandwidth of left sub block] /2)

(See also Figure "MSR signal structure" and Figure "Sub block definition".)

Suffix: 
<gap>
1 | 2

Gap channel number

Parameters:
<Spacing>
numeric value in Hz
*RST:2.5 MHz
