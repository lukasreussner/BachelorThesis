CALCulate<n>:MARKer<m>:LOEXclude <State>

This command turns the suppression of the local oscillator during automatic marker positioning on and off (for all markers in all windows).

Suffix: 
<n>, <m>

irrelevant

Parameters:
<State>
ON | OFF | 0 | 1
*RST:1
Example: CALC:MARK:LOEX ON
