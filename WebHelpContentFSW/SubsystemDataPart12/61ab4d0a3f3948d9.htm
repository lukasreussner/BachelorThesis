VSA (R&S FSW-K70) > Remote Commands for VSA > Importing and Exporting I/Q Data and Results > MMEMory:STORe:IQ:FORMat
MMEMory:STORe:IQ:FORMat <Format>,<DataFormat>

This command sets or queries the format of the I/Q data to be stored.

Parameters:
<Format>
FLOat32

32-bit floating point format.

INT32

32-bit integer format.

*RST:FLOat32
<DataFormat>
COMPlex

Exports complex data.

REAL

Exports real data.

*RST:COMPlex
Example: MMEM:STOR:IQ:FORM INT32,REAL
