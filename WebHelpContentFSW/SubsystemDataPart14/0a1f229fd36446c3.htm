Status Registers

The following commands are required for the status reporting system specific to the TD-SCDMA applications. In addition, the TD-SCDMA applications also use the standard status registers of the R&S FSW (depending on the measurement type).

For details and a description of the common R&S FSW status registers see Status Reporting System and STATus:QUEStionable:DIQ Register.

*RST does not influence the status registers.
