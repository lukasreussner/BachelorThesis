INPut<ip>:IQ:OSC:SRATe <SampleRate>

Returns the used oscilloscope acquisition sample rate, which depends on the used I/Q mode (see INPut<ip>:​IQ:​OSC:​TYPE).

Suffix: 
<ip>
1 | 2

irrelevant

Parameters:
<SampleRate>
10 GHz | 20 GHz
10 GHz

differential mode

20 GHz

single-ended mode

Default unit: HZ
Example: INP:IQ:OSC:SRAT?
