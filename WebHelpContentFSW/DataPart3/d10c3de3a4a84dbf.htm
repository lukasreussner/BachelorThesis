C/N0

Switches the measurement of the carrier/noise ratio with reference to a 1 Hz bandwidth on or off. If no marker is active, marker 1 is activated.

The measurement is performed on the trace that marker 1 is assigned to. To shift marker 1 and measure another trace, use the "Marker To Trace" softkey in the "Marker" menu (see "Assigning the Marker to a Trace").

Remote command: 

CALCulate<n>:​MARKer<m>:​FUNCtion:​POWer:​SELect

CALCulate<n>:​MARKer<m>:​FUNCtion:​POWer<sb>:​RESult?
