CALCulate<n>:MARKer<m>:FUNCtion:MDEPth[:STATe] <State>

This command turns the AM Modulation Depth measurement on and off.

To work correctly, the measurement requires an AM modulated signal.

Suffix: 
<n>

Window

<m>

Marker

Parameters:
<State>
ON | OFF | 1 | 0
*RST:0
