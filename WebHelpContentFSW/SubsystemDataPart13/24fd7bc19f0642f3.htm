Evaluation Range

Access: "Overview" > "Analysis" > "Evaluation Range"

or: [MEAS CONFIG] > "Evaluation Range"

The evaluation range defines which channel, slot or frame is evaluated in the result display.

For UE measurements, the branch to be evaluated can also be defined.
