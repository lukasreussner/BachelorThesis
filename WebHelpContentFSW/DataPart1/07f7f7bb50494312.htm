Use Settings For

Determines which channels the correction settings are applied to.

Note: In MSRA mode, the settings are always applied to all active measurement channels.

"All"

The frequency response correction settings are applied to all active measurement channels with the same input type. A new filter is calculated for each measurement channel as soon as you switch to it, or when a measurement is performed (e.g. by the Sequencer).

"Channel"

The frequency response correction settings are applied to the currently selected channel only.
