INPut<ip>:DIQ:RANGe[:UPPer] <Level>

Defines or queries the "Full Scale Level", i.e. the level that corresponds to an I/Q sample with the magnitude "1".

This command is only available if the optional Digital Baseband Interface is installed.

Suffix: 
<ip>
1 | 2

irrelevant

Parameters:
<Level>
Range: 1 μV  to  7.071 V
*RST:1 V
Default unit: DBM
