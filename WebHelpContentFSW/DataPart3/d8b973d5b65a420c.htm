RF INPUT 50 Ω

Connect a device under test (DUT) to the R&S FSW to provide RF input which is then analyzed. Connect the DUT to the RF INPUT on the R&S FSW via a cable equipped with an appropriate connector.

Risk of instrument damage

Do not overload the input. For maximum allowed values, see the data sheet. For AC-coupling, a DC input voltage of 50 V must never be exceeded. For DC-coupling, DC voltage must not be applied at the input. In both cases, noncompliance will destroy the input mixers.

Furthermore, for R&S FSW85 models, do not fasten the RF Input connector with a torque larger than 0.23 Nm. Rohde & Schwarz offers an appropriate torque wrench (R&S®ZN-ZTW Torque 0.23 Nm; delivered with the instrument).
