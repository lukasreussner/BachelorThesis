Result Summary

Displays the numerical measurement results for the demodulated signal components.

The scale bar at the bottom of the table provides a quick overview at a glance. It indicates the difference in depth of modulation (DDM) for ILS, and the azimuth (FROM/TO phase) for VOR measurements graphically.

For details on individual parameters, see "Avionics Parameters".

Note: If the result display is too narrow to display the complete table, the THD, K2 and K3 are hidden. Increase the width of the window to display the complete table.

Remote command: 

LAY:ADD? '1',RIGH,RSUM, see LAYout:​ADD[:​WINDow]?
