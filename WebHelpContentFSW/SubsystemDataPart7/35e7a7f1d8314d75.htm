CONFigure:SIGNal:ERRor:COMPensation:FOFFset:[STATe] <State>

This command turns compensation of the Frequency Offset on and off.

Available when you turn on CONFigure:SIGNal:ERRor:ESTimation:FOFFset:[STATe].

Parameters:
<State>
ON | OFF | 1 | 0
*RST:1
Example: CONF:SIGN:ERR:COMP:FOFF ON

Turns on error compensation.
