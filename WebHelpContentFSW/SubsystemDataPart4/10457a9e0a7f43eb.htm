DISPlay[:WINDow<n>]:TRACe<t>[:STATe] <State>

This command turns a trace on and off.

The measurement continues in the background.

Suffix: 
<n>

Window

<t>

Trace

Parameters:
<State>
ON | OFF | 1 | 0
*RST:1 for TRACe1, 0 for TRACe 2 to 6
Example: DISP:TRAC3 ON
