Alignment

Defines the alignment of the sidelobe range in relation to the "Peak Correlation" point.

"Left"

The sidelobe range stops to the left of the peak correlation point.

"Center"

The sidelobe range is centered around the peak correlation point.

"Right"

The sidelobe range starts to the right of the peak correlation point.

Remote command: 

SENSe:​TRACe:​MEASurement:​DEFine:​TSRange:​ALIGnment
