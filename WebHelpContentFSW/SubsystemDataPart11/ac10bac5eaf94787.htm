Frequency Deviation (Peak)

Maximum of Frequency Deviation vs Time trace.

All chirp frequency deviation table values are calculated from the time domain result:

fdev(k)= fmeas(k) - fideal(k)

For chirp start ≤ k ≤ chirp start + chirp length

Where:

fmeas(k): instantaneous frequency of the measured signal

fideal(k): ideal frequency trajectory obtained from weighted quadratic regression of the instantaneous signal phase φmeas (k) within the frequency measurement range (see "Hop / Chirp Measurement Settings ")

The peak deviation is thus defined as:

fdevpeak = max(|fdev(k)|)

for k∈ {frequency meas range}
