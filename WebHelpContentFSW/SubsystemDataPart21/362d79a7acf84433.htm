Table Configuration

Access: "Overview" ≥ "Result Config" > "Table Config"

or: MEAS CONFIG > "Result Config" > "Table Config"

You can configure which results are displayed in the following table results:
  • "Result Summary"
  • "Signal Content Detailed"
  • "Signal Content Summary (downstream only)"
  • "Bitstream (downstream only)"
  • "Synchronous Band Power (upstream only)"

However, the results are always calculated, regardless of their visibility on the screen.

For details on individual parameters see "Modulation Accuracy Parameters" and "Synchronous Band Power (upstream only)".

Remote command:

Result Summary only:

DISPlay[:​WINDow<n>]:​TABLe:​ITEM

Not available for Signal Content, Bitstream, and Synchronous Band Power results
