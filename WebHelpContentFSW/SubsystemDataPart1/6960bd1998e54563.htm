Average Power Consumption

The Analog Baseband interface can be used to capture two different signals: one proportional to the voltage and one proportional to the current of a DUT. The average power consumption can then be calculated from the captured I/Q signal. To avoid processing large amounts of I/Q data, the R&S FSW provides an internal calculation of the average power consumption for remote operation according to the following equation:

P(n) = A * V(n) * I(n) - B * V(n) * V(n)

with:

  • V(n): I data of the instrument
  • I(n): Q data of the instrument
  • A: conversion factor A
  • B: conversion factor B
Remote commands:

TRACe:​IQ:​APCon[:​STATe]

TRACe:​IQ:​APCon:​A

TRACe:​IQ:​APCon:​B
