Modulation

Defines the modulation used by the specified subcarriers.

For the NCP profile, zero bit modulation is used for all entries except the index 0, whose modulation depends on the "NCP Modulation" setting and cannot be edited here.

Remote command: 

CONFigure:​DS:​CHANnel:​PCONfig<pri>:​SUBCarrier:​MODulation
