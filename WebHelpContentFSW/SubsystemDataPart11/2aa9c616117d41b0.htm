Reference

Defines the reference point for positioning the frequency/phase/power measurement range. The "Offset Begin / Offset End" is given with respect to this value.

For details on the measurement range parameters see "Measurement Range".

"Edge"

The measurement range is defined in reference to the rising or falling edge.

"Center"

The measurement range is defined in reference to the center of the hop/chirp.

"FM Settling"

The measurement range starts at the FM settling point (see "FM settling point").
