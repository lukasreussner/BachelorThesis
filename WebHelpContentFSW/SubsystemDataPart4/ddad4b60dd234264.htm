Magnitude Capture Results

The Magnitude Capture trace consists of 32001 trace values, regardless of the defined capture time and thus of the length of the capture buffer.

To retrieve the (complete) captured I/Q data, use the MMEM:STOR:IQ:STAT command, see MMEMory:​STORe<n>:​IQ:​STATe.
