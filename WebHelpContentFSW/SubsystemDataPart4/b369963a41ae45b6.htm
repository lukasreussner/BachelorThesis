CONFigure[:MS]:POWer:PCARrier:AUTO <State>

If enabled, the maximum measured power level for the carriers is used as the maximum output power per carrier.

If disabled, the maximum power is defined by CONFigure[:​MS]:​POWer:​PCARrier.

Parameters:
<State>
ON | OFF | 1 | 0
*RST:1
Example: CONF:POW:PCAR:AUTO OFF
CONF:POW:PCAR 4 dBm
Manual operation: See "Maximum Output Power per Carrier (multicarrier measurements only)"
