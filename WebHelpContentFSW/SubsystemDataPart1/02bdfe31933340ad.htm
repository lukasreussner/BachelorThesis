Microbutton Action

Active Rohde & Schwarz probes (except for R&S RT-ZS10E) have a configurable microbutton on the probe head. By pressing this button, you can perform an action on the instrument directly from the probe.

Select the action that you want to start from the probe:

"Run Single"

Starts one data acquisition.

"No Action"

Prevents unwanted actions due to unintended usage of the microbutton.

Remote command: 

[SENSe:​]PROBe<p>:​SETup:​MODE
