Pulse Measurements (R&S FSW-K6/6S) > Remote Commands for Pulse Measurements > Data Acquisition > [SENSe:]BANDwidth:DEMod
[SENSe:]BANDwidth:DEMod <Bandwidth>
[SENSe:]BWIDth:DEMod <Bandwidth>

Sets/queries the measurement bandwidth in Hz.

The measurement bandwidth is defined by the used filter and the sample rate. For information on supported sample rates and filter bandwidths see the data sheet.

Parameters:
<Bandwidth>
*RST:80.0 MHz
Default unit: HZ
