CONFigure:US:CHANnel:SYMBols <SymbolsPerFrame>

Defines the number of symbols per frame to be expected.

Setting parameters: 
<SymbolsPerFrame>

The number of symbols per frame varies depending on the used bandwidth and "NFFT (FFT length)".

Range: 2K mode: 6 to 36; 4K mode: 6 to 18;
Example: CONF:US:CHAN:SYMB 8
Manual operation: See "Symbols Per Frame (K)"
