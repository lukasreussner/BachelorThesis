Phase Noise (R&S FSW-K40) > Remote Control Commands for Phase Noise Measurements > Controlling the Measurement > [SENSe:]IQ:DPLL
[SENSe:]IQ:DPLL <State>

This command turns the digital PLL on and off.

Parameters:
<State>
ON | OFF | 1 | 0
Example: IQ:DPLL ON

Turns the digital PLL on.

Manual operation: See "Digital PLL"
