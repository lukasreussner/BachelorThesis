Power vs Symbol

The Power vs Symbol evaluation shows the power measured for each symbol in the selected channel and the selected slot. The power is not averaged here.

Depending on the spreading factor (symbol rate) of the channel, a slot can contain a minimum of 44 and a maximum of 704 symbols (see Table "Channel parameters and their dependencies").

Remote command: 

LAY:ADD? '1',RIGH, PSYMbol, see LAYout:​ADD[:​WINDow]?
