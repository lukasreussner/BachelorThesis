UNIT:SFLatness <Unit>

This command switches between relative (dB) and absolute (dBm) results for Spectrum Flatness results (see "Spectrum Flatness").

Parameters:
<Unit>
DB | DBM
*RST:DBM
Example: UNIT:SFL DBM
Manual operation: See "Units"
