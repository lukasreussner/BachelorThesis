Tx Channel Bandwidth

The Tx channel bandwidth is normally defined by the transmission technology standard. The correct bandwidth is predefined automatically for the selected technology. Each Tx channel is defined independently of the others; automatic bandwidth configuration for subsequent channels as in common ACLR measurements is not performed.

The bandwidth for each channel is indicated by a colored bar in the display.

Remote command: 

[SENSe:​]POWer:​ACHannel:​SBLock<sb>:​BANDwidth[:​CHANnel<ch>]
