Measurement 1: Measuring the Signal Channel Power

The measurement of the spectrum gives an overview of the 3GPP FDD BTS signal and the spurious emissions close to the carrier.

Test setup
► 

Connect the RF output of the R&S SMW200A to the RF input of the R&S FSW (coaxial cable with N connectors).

Settings on the R&S SMW200A

  1. PRESET

  2. "FREQ" = 2.1175 GHz

  3. "LEVEL"= 0 dBm

  4. "BASEBAND A > CDMA Standards > 3GPP FDD"

  5. "General" tab: "LINK DIRECTION > DOWN/FORWARD"

  6. "Base station" tab: "TEST MODELS > Test_Model_1_16_channels"

  7. "Base station" tab: "Select Base station > BS 1 > ON"

  8. "General" tab: "3GPP FDD > STATE > ON"

Settings on the R&S FSW

  1. PRESET

  2. "MODE > 3GPP FDD BTS"

  3. "AMPT > Reference level"= 0 dBm

  4. "FREQ > Center frequency" = 2.1175 GHz

  5. "MEAS > POWER"

  6. "AMPT > Scale Config > Auto Scale Once"
