Amplifier

The Amplifier Measurement application requires an instrument equipped with the Amplifier Measurement option R&S FSW-K18. This application provides measurements to measure the efficiency of traditional amplifiers and amplifiers that support envelope tracking with the R&S FSW.

Also available is option R&S FSW-K18D, which provides direct DPD functionality.

For details refer to See Also.

Remote command: 

INST:SEL AMPL, see INSTrument[:​SELect]
