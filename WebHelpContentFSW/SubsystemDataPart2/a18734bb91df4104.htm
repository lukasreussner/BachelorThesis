Configuring and Performing Sweeps

When the Pulse application is activated, a continuous sweep is performed automatically. However, you can stop and start a new measurement any time.

Furthermore, you can perform a sequence of measurements using the Sequencer (see "Multiple Measurement Channels and Sequencer Function").

Useful commands for configuring sweeps described elsewhere:
  • [SENSe:​]SWEep:​TIME
  • [SENSe:​]SWEep:​POINts
