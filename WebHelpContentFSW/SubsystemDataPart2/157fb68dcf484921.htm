Pulse I and Q

Displays the magnitude of the I and Q components of the selected pulse versus time as separate traces in one diagram. The length and alignment of the trace can be configured in the "Result Range" dialog box (see "Result Range").

Remote command: 

LAY:ADD:WIND '2',RIGH,PIAQ see LAYout:​ADD[:​WINDow]?

Results:

[SENSe:​]PULSe:​POWer:​AMPLitude:​I?

[SENSe:​]PULSe:​POWer:​AMPLitude:​Q?
