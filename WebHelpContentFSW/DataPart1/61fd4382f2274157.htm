Bands

Defines the frequency range of the bands used by the base station.

This setting is only available for "Band Category" 1 or 3.

This setting is required to calculate the SEM limits according to standard 3GPP 37.141 V12.2.0.

Remote command: 

[SENSe:​]ESPectrum<sb>:​MSR:​BAND
