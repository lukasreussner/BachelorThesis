Absolute and Relative Values (ACLR Mode)

The powers of the adjacent channels are output in dBm or dBm/Hz (absolute values), or in dBc, relative to the specified reference Tx channel.

"Abs"

The absolute power in the adjacent channels is displayed in the unit of the y-axis, e.g. in dBm, dBµV.

"Rel"

The level of the adjacent channels is displayed relative to the level of the transmission channel in dBc.

Remote command: 

[SENSe:​]POWer:​ACHannel:​MODE
