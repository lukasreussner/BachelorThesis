[SENSe:]POWer:ACHannel:FILTer:ALPHa:CHANnel<ch> <Alpha>

This command defines the roll-off factor for the transmission channel weighting filter.

Suffix: 
<ch>
1 to 18

Tx channel number

Parameters:
<Alpha>

Roll-off factor

Range: 0  to  1
