Result Summary

The result summary provides measurement results based on the complete captured signal.

Note: You can configure which results are displayed (see "Table Configuration"). However, the results are always calculated, regardless of their visibility.

For details on the individual results and the summarized values see "Modulation Accuracy Parameters".
