Tuning Modes

Basically, the application calculates the noise figure of a DUT based on the characteristics of the DUT that have been measured and a noise source whose properties are known. Therefore, the [Noise Source Control] connector on the R&S FSW is a prerequisite for the R&S FSW Noise measurements application.

In addition to the noise characteristics of a DUT, the application is also able to determine several more DUT characteristics like its gain or its noise temperature characteristics.

The application provides several measurement modes or tuning modes.
