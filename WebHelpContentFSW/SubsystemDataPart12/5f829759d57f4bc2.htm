VSA (R&S FSW-K70) > Configuration > Signal Capture > Sweep Settings > Continue Single Sweep
Continue Single Sweep

After triggering, repeats the number of evaluations set in "Statistics Count" , without deleting the trace of the last measurement.

While the measurement is running, the "Continue Single Sweep" softkey and the [RUN SINGLE] key are highlighted. The running measurement can be aborted by selecting the highlighted softkey or key again.

Remote command: 

INITiate<n>:​CONMeas
