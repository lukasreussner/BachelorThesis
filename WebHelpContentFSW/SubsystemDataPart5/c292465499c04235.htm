Problem: No identification signal results at all in ILS Result Summary

A demodulation bandwidth of 800 Hz does not allow for identification signals to be demodulated. Select a larger demodulation bandwidth (see "Data Acquisition and Detection").

Explanation: The maximum AF frequency that can be analyzed is 0.5 * Demodulation BW = 400 Hz (for carrier offset = 0 Hz and DBW = 800 Hz). However, the identification/voice signal is 300 Hz to 4000 Hz, typically 1020 Hz.
