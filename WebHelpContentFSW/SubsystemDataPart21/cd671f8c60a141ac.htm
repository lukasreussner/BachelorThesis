UNIT:POWer <Unit>

Determines whether the power results in the Result Summary are calculated as absolute values or relative to the power of the data subcarriers.

Parameters:
<Unit>
ABSolute

Power results are provided as absolute values. The unit depends on the CALCulate<n>:​UNIT:​POWer command.

RELative

Power results are provided relative to the power measured in the data subcarriers.

*RST:REL
Example: UNIT:POW ABS
CALC:UNIT:POW DBM

Sets the power unit to absolute values in dBm.

Manual operation: See "Power Unit"
