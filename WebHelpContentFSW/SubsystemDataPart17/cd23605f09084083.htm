Symbol Constellation

The "Symbol Constellation" evaluation shows all modulated symbols of the selected channel and the selected slot.

The BTS application supports BPSK, QPSK, 8PSK, 16QAM and 64QAM modulation types. The modulation type itself depends on the channel type. Refer to "Channel Type Characteristics" for further information.

Note: QPSK constellation points are located on the diagonals (not x and y-axis) of the constellation diagram. BPSK constellation points are always on the x-axis.

The number of symbols is in the range from 1 to 100, depending on the symbol rate of the channel (see "Channel Type Characteristics").
