[SENSe:]ESPectrum<sb>:RANGe<range>:INSert <Mode>

This command inserts a new SEM range and updates the range numbers accordingly.

Suffix: 
<sb>
1 | 2 | 3

Sub block in a Multi-SEM measurement

<range>
1..30

Selects the SEM range.

Parameters:
<Mode>
AFTer

Inserts a range after the selected range.

BEFore

Inserts a range before the selected range.

See "Example: SEM Measurement".
