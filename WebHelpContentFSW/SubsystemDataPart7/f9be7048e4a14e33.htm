Selecting the AM/PM definition

The "AM/PM Definition" feature selects the way the AM/PM results are calculated.

In effect, the "Meas - Ref" method is the inverse of the "Ref - Meas" method, which is the default.

Remote command: 

CALCulate<n>:​AMPM:​DEFinition
