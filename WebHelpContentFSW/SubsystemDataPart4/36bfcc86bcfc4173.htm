ARFCN

Defines the Absolute Radio Frequency Channel Number (ARFCN). The "Center Frequency" is adapted accordingly.

Possible values are in the range from 0 to 1023; however, some values may not be allowed depending on the selected "Frequency Band".

Remote command: 

CONFigure[:​MS]:​ARFCn
