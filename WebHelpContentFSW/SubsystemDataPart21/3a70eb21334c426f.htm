CONFigure:DS:CHANnel:NCP:PCONfig<pri>:SUBCarrier:STOP <SCStop>

Defines the last subcarrier in a series of subcarriers in the selected row to be configured identically in the NCP profile.

Suffix: 
<pri>
1..n
Parameters:
<SCStop>
Range: 1  to  2K mode: 2047; 4K mode: 4095

Must be higher than the parameter used by CONFigure:​DS:​CHANnel:​NCP:​PCONfig<pri>:​SUBCarrier:​STARt.

Example: CONF:DS:CHAN:NCP:PCON2:SUBC:STOP 100
Manual operation: See "Start/ Increment/ Stop"
