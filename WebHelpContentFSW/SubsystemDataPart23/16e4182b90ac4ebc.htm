CONFigure[:LTE]:UL[:CC<cc>]:SRS:ANST <State>

This command turns simultaneous transmission of the Sounding Reference Signal (SRS) and ACK/NACK messages (via PUCCH) on and off.

Simultaneous transmission works only if the PUCCH format ist either 1, 1a, 1b or 3.

Suffix: 
<cc>

Component Carrier

Parameters:
<State>
ON

Allows simultaneous transmission of SRS and PUCCH.

OFF

SRS not transmitted in the subframe for which you have configured simultaneous transmission of PUCCH and SRS.

Example: CONF:UL:SRS:ANST ON

Turns simultaneous transmission of the SRS and PUCCH in one subframe on.

Manual operation: See "A/N + SRS Simultaneous TX"
