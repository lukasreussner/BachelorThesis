Demodulation - Compensation and Equalizer

Access: "Overview" > "Demodulation" > "Demodulation"

or: [MEAS CONFIG] > "Demod/Meas Filter" > "Demodulation" tab

During demodulation of the vector signal some undesired effects that may occur during transmission can be compensated for.

A live preview of the constellation with the current settings is displayed in the preview area at the bottom of the dialog box. The preview area is not editable directly.

Demodulation settings depend on the used modulation.
