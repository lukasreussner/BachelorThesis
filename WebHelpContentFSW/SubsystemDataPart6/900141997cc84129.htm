CALibration:MCGD

This command initiates a new calibration. You can synchronize to the end of the measurement as usual with *OPC, *OPC? or *WAI.

Example: CAL:MCGD;*WAI

Performs a calibration measurement and waits for the results.

Usage: Event
Manual operation: See "Calibrate (Execute Reference Calibration)"
