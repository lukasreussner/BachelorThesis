[SENSe:]CORRection:ENR[:MEASurement]:SPOT:COLD <Temperature>

This command defines a constant temperature of a resistor not supplied with power (Tcold) used during measurements.

The command is available when you have selected a noise source with resistor characteristics with [SENSe:]CORRection:ENR[:MEASurement]:TYPE.

Parameters:
<Temperature>

Temperature in degrees Kelvin.

*RST:77 K
Example: CORR:ENR:TYPE RES
CORR:ENR:SPOT:COLD 100

Defines a cold temperature of 100 K.

Manual operation: See "Measurement"
