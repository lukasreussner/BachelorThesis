Remove Residual Spurs

If enabled, residual spurs, which are generated by internal components in the R&S FSW itself, are not included in the spur results. Note, however, if a residual spur coincides with a "true" spur, the spur may also be removed.

On the other hand, some residuals detected in the spectral overview sweep might "disappear" from the final spur results even if the "Remove Residual Spurs" setting is disabled, due to the different measurement parameters for the spectral overview sweep and the spot search.

Remote command: 

[SENSe:​]SSEarch:​RREMove
