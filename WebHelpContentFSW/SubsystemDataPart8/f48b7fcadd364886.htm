[SENSe:]CONFigure:MEASurement <Measurement>

This command selects the type of power measurement to perform next.

The command is available for manual measurements (see [SENSe:]CONFigure:CONTrol).

Parameters:
<Measurement>
COLD

Performs the Power (Cold) measurement next.

HOT

Performs the Power (Hot) measurement next.

Example: CONF:CONT MAN
CONF:MEAS HOT

Performs the hot power measurement.

Manual operation: See "Meas Mode (Auto Manual)"
