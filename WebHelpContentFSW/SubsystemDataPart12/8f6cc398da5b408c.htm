INPut<ip>:GAIN[:VALue] <Gain>

This command selects the gain if the preamplifier is activated (INP:GAIN:STAT ON, see INPut<ip>:​GAIN:​STATe).

The command requires the additional preamplifier hardware option.

Suffix: 
<ip>
1 | 2

irrelevant

Parameters:
<Gain>
15 dB | 30 dB

The availability of gain levels depends on the model of the R&S FSW.

R&S FSW8/13: 15dB and 30 dB

R&S FSW26 or higher: 30 dB

All other values are rounded to the nearest of these two.

Default unit: DB
Example: INP:GAIN:STAT ON
INP:GAIN:VAL 30

Switches on 30 dB preamplification.

Manual operation: See "Preamplifier"
