Pattern Configuration

Access: "Meas Config" > "Pattern Config"

For common signal standards, the patterns to be searched for in the captured signal are predefined in the VSA application. In addition, new patterns can be defined and assigned to a signal standard manually.

For details on patterns see "Overview of the Demodulation Process".
