Adapting the limit lines for wideband noise (Apply Exceptions)

If enabled, exceptions from the limit line check as defined in the 3GPP standard are applied to the limit checks of the MCWN measurements.

Remote command: 

CONFigure:​SPECtrum:​LIMit:​EXCeption[:​STATe]
