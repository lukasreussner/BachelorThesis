Activating MSRA Measurements

MSRA measurements requires a special operating mode on the R&S FSW. A measurement is started immediately with the default settings.

The special MSRA Master measurement channel is of the channel type "IQ" and is referred to by the channel name "MSRA Master". This channel cannot be replaced, deleted, or renamed.
