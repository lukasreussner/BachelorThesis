Symbol Constellation

The "Symbol Constellation" evaluation shows all modulated symbols of the selected channel and the selected PCG.

The BTS application supports BPSK, QPSK, 8PSK and 16QAM modulation types. The modulation type itself depends on the channel type. Refer to "BTS Channel Types" for further information.

Note: QPSK constellation points are located on the diagonals (not x and y-axis) of the constellation diagram. BPSK constellation points are always on the x-axis.

The number of symbols is in the range from 6 (min) to 384 (max), depending on the symbol rate of the channel (see "Measurement Basics").
