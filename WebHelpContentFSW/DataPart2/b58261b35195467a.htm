Carrier Adjacent to RF Bandwidth Edge

For particular measurement setups, the specification demands specific limits for the SEM ranges.

These settings are only available for "Band Category" 2.

For details, see "Multi-Standard Radio (MSR) SEM Measurements".

"GSM/Edge Present"

A GSM/EDGE carrier is located at the edge of the RF band.

In this case, the power of the GSM carrier must be specified (see "Power Gsm Carrier").

"LTE FDD 1.4MHz/3MHz Present"

An LTE FDD 1.4 MHz or 3 MHz carrier is located at the edge of the RF band.

Remote command: 

[SENSe:​]ESPectrum<sb>:​MSR:​GSM:​CPResent

[SENSe:​]ESPectrum<sb>:​MSR:​LTE:​CPResent
