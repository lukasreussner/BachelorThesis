Subcarrier Range(Start/ Increment/ Stop)

Defines a series of subcarriers to be configured identically.

The following restrictions apply:
  • "Start" < "Stop"
  • "Increment" ≥ 1
For example: to configure all 10 carriers between subcarriers 2044 and 2053, define:
  • "Start" = 2044
  • "Increment" = 1
  • "Stop" = 2053
Remote command: 

CONFigure:​DS:​CHANnel:​CPES<i>:​SUBCarrier:​STARt

CONFigure:​DS:​CHANnel:​CPES<i>:​SUBCarrier:​INCRement

CONFigure:​DS:​CHANnel:​CPES<i>:​SUBCarrier:​STOP
