Demodulation Settings

Access: "Overview" > "Demodulation"

or: [MEAS CONFIG] > "Demod/Meas Filter" > "Demodulation" tab

During demodulation of the vector signal some undesired effects that may occur during transmission can be compensated for. Furthermore, you can influence the synchronization process.

The advanced demodulation settings are described in "Advanced Demodulation (Synchronization)".

The measurement filter settings are described in "Measurement Filter Settings".
