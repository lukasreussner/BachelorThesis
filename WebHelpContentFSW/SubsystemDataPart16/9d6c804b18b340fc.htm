CONFigure:CDPower[:BTS]:MCARrier[:STATe] <State>

This command activates or deactivates the multicarrier mode.

Parameters:
<State>
ON | OFF | 1 | 0
*RST:0
Example: CONF:CDP:MCAR ON

Activates the multicarrier settings.

Manual operation: See "Multicarrier"
