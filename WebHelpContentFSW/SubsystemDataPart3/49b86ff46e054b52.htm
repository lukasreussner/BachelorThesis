[SENSe:]AVERage<n>:TYPE <Mode>

This command selects the trace averaging mode.

Suffix: 
<n>

Window

Parameters:
<Mode>
VIDeo

The logarithmic power values are averaged.

LINear

The power values are averaged before they are converted to logarithmic values.

POWer

The power level values are converted into unit Watt prior to averaging. After the averaging, the data is converted back into its original unit.

*RST:VIDeo
Example: AVER:TYPE LIN

Switches to linear average calculation.
