[SENSe:]IQ:BANDwidth:MODE <Mode>
[SENSe:]IQ:BWIDth:MODE <Mode>

This command defines how the resolution bandwidth is determined.

Parameters:
<Mode>
AUTO | MANual | FFT
AUTO

(Default) The RBW is determined automatically depending on the sample rate and record length.

MANual

The user-defined RBW is used and the (FFT) window length (and possibly the sample rate) are adapted accordingly. The RBW is defined using the [SENSe:​]IQ:​BWIDth:​RESolution command.

FFT

The RBW is determined by the FFT parameters.

*RST:AUTO
Example: IQ:BAND:MODE MAN

Switches to manual RBW mode.

IQ:BAND:RES 120000

Sets the RBW to 120 kHz.

Manual operation: See "RBW"
