[SENSe:]LIST:RANGe<range>:INPut:GAIN[:VALue] <Gain>

This command selects the preamplification level for the range.

The command requires option R&S FSW-B24.

Suffix: 
<range>
1..30

Selects the measurement range.

Parameters:
<Gain>
15 dB | 30 dB

The availability of preamplification levels depends on the R&S FSW model.

• R&S FSW8/13: 15dB and 30 dB

• R&S FSW26 or higher: 30 dB

All other values are rounded to the nearest of these two.

*RST:OFF
