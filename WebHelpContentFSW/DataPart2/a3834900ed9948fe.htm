CALCulate<n>:MARKer<m>:FUNCtion:POWer[:STATe] <State>

This command turns a power measurement on and off.

Suffix: 
<n>, <m>

irrelevant

Parameters:
<State>
ON | OFF | 1 | 0
ON | 1

The power measurement selected with CALCulate<n>:​MARKer<m>:​FUNCtion:​POWer:​SELect is activated.

OFF | 0

A standard frequency sweep is activated.

*RST:0
