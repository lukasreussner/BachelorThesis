[SENSe:]CDPower:MMAX <ModType>

This command defines the highest modulation to be considered in the automatic channel search. In low SNR environments it may be necessary to limit the channel search to lower modulations than 64QAM.

Parameters:
<ModType>
QPSK

Consider QPSK modulation only

PSK8

Consider QPSK and 8PSK modulation.

QAM16

Consider QPSK, 8PSK and 16QAM modulation

QAM64

Consider QPSK, 8PSK, 16QAM and 64QAM modulation

*RST:QAM64
Example: SENS:CDP:MMAX PSK8

Assume QPSK and 8PSK modulations only for the automatic channel search
