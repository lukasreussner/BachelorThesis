Gain Deviation vs Time

The Gain Deviation vs Time result display shows the deviation of each measured signal sample from the average gain of the measured signal.

The x-axis shows the time in seconds. The y-axis shows the gain deviation in dB.

The displayed results are based on the synchronized measurement data (represented by the green bar in the Capture Buffer).

Note that the result query and trace export only work in case of unencrypted reference signal waveform files.

Remote command: 

Selection: LAY:ADD GDVT

Result query: TRACe<t>[:​DATA]?
