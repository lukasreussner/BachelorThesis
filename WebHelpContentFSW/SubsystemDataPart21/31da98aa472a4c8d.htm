DISPlay[:WINDow<n>]:BITStream:LAYout <View>

Determines whether a compact or expanded view of the bits are displayed in the Bitstream result display, if activated (see "Bitstream (downstream only)").

Suffix: 
<n>

Window

Parameters:
<View>
COMPact | EXPanded
Manual operation: See "Bitstream Layout"
