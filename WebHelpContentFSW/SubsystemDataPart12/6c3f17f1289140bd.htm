Problem: The trace of the measurement signal is visible in the measurement window; the trace of the reference signal is not
Solution:
  • 1.Select the measurement window.
  • 2. Press the [TRACE] key.
  • 3. Press the "Trace Config" softkey.
  • 4. Select a second trace, choose "Clear Write" as "Trace Mode" and toggle to "Ref" in the "Evaluation" column.
