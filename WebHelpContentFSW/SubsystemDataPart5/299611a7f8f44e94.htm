Avionics (VOR/ILS) Measurements application (R&S FSW-15) > Remote Commands to Perform VOR/ILS Measurements > Analyzing VOR/ILS Measurements > Configuring the Y-Axis Scaling and Units > UNIT<n>:THD
UNIT<n>:THD <THDUnit>

Switches units between dB and percent for the total harmonic distortion (THD), K2 and K3 results in the "Distortion Summary" and "Result Summary" and the corresponding remote commands.

Suffix: 
<n>
1..n

irrelevant

Parameters:
<THDUnit>
DB | PCT
*RST:PCT
Example: UNIT:THD DB
Manual operation: See "Distortion"
