Alignment Signal Source (Option R&S FSW-B2000)

The alignment signal source is required to align the connected oscilloscope and the oscilloscope ADC for the optional 2 GHz bandwidth extension (R&S FSW-B2000).

For details, see "IF and Video Signal Output".
