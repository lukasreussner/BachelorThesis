Number of Slots to measure

This parameter specifies the "Number of Slots to measure" for the measurement interval of multi-slot measurements, i.e. the Power vs Time and Transient Spectrum measurements. Between 1 and 8 consecutive slots can be measured.

See also "Defining the Scope of the Measurement".

Remote command: 

CONFigure[:​MS]:​CHANnel:​MSLots:​NOFSlots
