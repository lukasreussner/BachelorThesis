Magnitude Capture

Displays the power vs. time trace of the captured I/Q data.

Pre-trigger samples are not displayed.

The analyzed slot scopes (1 to 8 slots of a single GSM frame) are indicated by a green bar, the "Slot to Measure" in each frame by a blue bar at the bottom of the diagram.

For details see "Defining the Scope of the Measurement".

For negative trigger offsets, the trigger is displayed as a vertical red line labeled "TRG".

Remote command: 

LAY:ADD:WIND '2',RIGH,MCAP see LAYout:​ADD[:​WINDow]?

Results:

FETCh:​MCAPture:​SLOTs:​SCOPe?

FETCh:​MCAPture:​SLOTs:​MEASure?

TRACe<n>[:​DATA]?
