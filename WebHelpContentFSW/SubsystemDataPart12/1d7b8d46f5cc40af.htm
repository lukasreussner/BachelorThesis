Limit and Display Lines

Access: [LINES] > "Lines/Limits"

The results of a modulation accuracy measurement can be checked for violation of defined limits automatically (see "Modulation Accuracy").

For eye diagrams, display lines allow you to measure the size of the eye in the diagram.
