X-Axis

Selects the frequency data that is displayed on the x-axis.

For measurements on frequency converting DUTs with a variable intermediary frequency, you can display either the RF frequency or the IF frequency.

Note that a change of the x-axis scale applies to all result displays.

The "Frequency Axis" scale is also available via the [FREQ] key.

Remote command: 

DISPlay[:​WINDow<n>]:​TRACe:​X[:​SCALe]
