Conflict

Indicates whether a conflict between entries in the table has occurred. In particular, the frequency spans must be distinct, that is: they may not overlap. Conflicting settings are also indicated in red.

Tip: For manually defined frequencies, it may be useful to sort the entries by frequency in order to detect overlapping frequency spans. See "Sort Table by Frequency".
