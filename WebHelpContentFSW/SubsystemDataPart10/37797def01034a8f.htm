Noise Floor Estimate

Displays the noise floor estimated during the "Spectral Overview". This information helps you understand the RBWs used for the individual segments by the R&S FSW Spurious measurements application.

Remote command: 

LAY:ADD? '1',RIGH,NESTimate, see LAYout:​ADD[:​WINDow]?
