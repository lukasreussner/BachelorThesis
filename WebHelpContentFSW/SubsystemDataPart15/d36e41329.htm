[SENSe:]CDPower:ANTenna <AntennaState>

This command deactivates the orthogonal transmit diversity (two-antenna system) or defines the antenna for which the result display is evaluated.

For details on antenna diversity see also "Antenna Diversity".

Parameters:
<AntennaState>
OFF | 1 | 2
OFF

The aggregate signal from both antennas is fed in.

1

The signal of antenna 1 is fed in.

2

The signal of antenna 2 is fed in.

*RST:OFF

For further details refer to "Antenna Diversity - Antenna Number".

Example: CDP:ANT 2

Selects antenna 2.

Manual operation: See "Antenna Diversity - Antenna Number"
