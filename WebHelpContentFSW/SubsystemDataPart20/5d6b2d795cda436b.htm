Spectrum Emission Mask

Access: "Overview" > "Select Measurement" > "SEM"

Or: [MEAS] > "Select Measurement" > "SEM"

The Spectrum Emission Mask measurement shows the quality of the measured signal by comparing the power values in the frequency range near the carrier against a spectral mask that is defined by the IEEE 802.11ad specifications. Thus, the performance of the DUT can be tested and the emissions and their distance to the limit are identified.

Note that the IEEE 802.11ad standard does not distinguish between spurious and spectral emissions.

The Result Summary contains a peak list with the values for the largest spectral emissions including their frequency and power.

  • Sweep time

The main measurement menus for the SEM measurements are identical to the Spectrum application.
