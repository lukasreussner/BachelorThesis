Sequence Hopping

Turns sequence hopping for the uplink demodulation reference signal on and off.

Sequence hopping is generated by a pseudo-random sequence generator.

Remote command: 

CONFigure[:​LTE]:​UL[:​CC<cc>]:​DRS:​SEQHopping
