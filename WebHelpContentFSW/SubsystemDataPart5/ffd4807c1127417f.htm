Problem: Modulation Spectrum display shows strange distortion products (VOR measurement)

Possible Solution: Could be FM to AM conversion, caused by FM signals or FM-like distortion products falling onto the application's digital filter's slope. Increase the Demodulation BW so that the critical frequencies fall into the filter's flat passband. Also check the measured carrier offset and adjust the carrier frequency setting either in the R&S FSW Avionics (VOR/ILS) measurements application or on the DUT.
