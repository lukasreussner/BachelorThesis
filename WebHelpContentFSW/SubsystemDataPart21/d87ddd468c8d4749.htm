Numeric Results for Frequency Sweep Measurements

The following commands are required to retrieve the numeric results of the DOCSIS 3.1 frequency sweep measurements (see "Frequency Sweep Measurements".

In the following commands used to retrieve the numeric results for RF data, the suffixes <n> for CALCulate and <k> for LIMit are irrelevant.

List of Commands
CALCulate<n>:MARKer<m>:FUNCtion:POWer:RESult?
CALCulate<n>:MARKer<m>:X
CALCulate<n>:STATistics:RESult<t>?
