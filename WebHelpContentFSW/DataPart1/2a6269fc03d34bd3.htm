EXT MIXER Connector (Optional)

Connect external mixers to the EXT MIXER LO OUT/IF IN and IF IN female connectors to increase the available frequency range. These connectors are optional and only available with R&S FSW-B21.

If no external mixers are connected to the R&S FSW, cover the two front connectors LO OUT / IF IN and IF IN with the supplied SMA caps.
