TRACe:IQ:SRATe:AUTO <State>

This command turns automatic selection of an appropriate (capture) sample rate on and off.

When you turn on this feature, the application calculates an appropriate sample rate based on the reference signal and adjusts the other data acquisition settings accordingly.

Parameters:
<State>
ON | OFF | 1 | 0
*RST:1
Example: TRAC:IQ:SRAT:AUTO ON

Selects automatic adjustment of the sample rate.

Manual operation: See "Automatic adjustment"
