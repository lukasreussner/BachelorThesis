CALCulate<n>:DISTribution:LLINes[:STATe] <State>

Hides or shows the limit lines in the selected Parameter Trend or Parameter Distribution result display.

Note that this function only has an effect on the visibility of the lines in the graphical displays, it does not affect the limit check in general or the display of the limit check results in the table displays.

Suffix: 
<n>

Window

Parameters:
<State>
ON | OFF | 1 | 0
*RST:1
Example: CALC:DIST:LLIN ON
Manual operation: See "Display Limit Lines"
