Radio Frequency Input

Access: "Overview" > "Input/Frontend" > "Input Source" > "Radio Frequency"

Or: INPUT/OUTPUT > "Input Source Config" > "Input Source" > "Radio Frequency"

The only input source for the R&S FSW Avionics (VOR/ILS) measurements application is "Radio Frequency", i.e. the signal at the RF INPUT connector of the R&S FSW.
