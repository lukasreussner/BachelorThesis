EVM Calculation Method

Selects the method to calculate the EVM.

  • EVM 3GPP Definition
    Calculation of the EVM according to 3GPP TS 36.141. Evaluates the EVM at two trial timing positions and then uses the maximum EVM of the two.
  • At Optimal Timing Position
    Calculates the EVM using the optimal timing position.
Remote command: 

[SENSe:​][LTE:​]DL:​DEMod:​EVMCalc
