In-Phase Amplitude/Quadrature Amplitude

The pulse in-phase or quadrature amplitude as a voltage, measured at the measurement point of the pulse (see "Measurement Point"). Values range from -10 mV to +10 mV.

Remote command: 

Querying results:

[SENSe:​]PULSe:​POWer:​AMPLitude:​I?

[SENSe:​]PULSe:​POWer:​AMPLitude:​Q?

CALCulate<n>:​TABLe:​POWer:​AMPLitude:​I

CALCulate<n>:​TABLe:​POWer:​AMPLitude:​Q

Querying limit check results:

[SENSe:​]PULSe:​POWer:​AMPLitude:​I:​LIMit?

[SENSe:​]PULSe:​POWer:​AMPLitude:​Q:​LIMit?
