VOR Parameters

For VOR measurements, the following parameters are determined.

└ 30 Hz AM depth
└ 30 Hz AM frequency
└ 30 Hz AM THD
└ 9.96 kHz AM depth
└ 9.96 kHz AM frequency
└ 9.96 kHz AM THD
└ 30 Hz FM depth
└ 30 Hz FM frequency
└ 30 Hz FM THD
└ VOICE / IDENT AM depth
└ VOICE / IDENT AM frequency
└ VOICE / IDENT AM THD
└ VOR Phase
└ K2
└ K3
└ THD
