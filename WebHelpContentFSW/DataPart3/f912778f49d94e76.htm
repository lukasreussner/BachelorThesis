EMI Measurement Configuration

Access: "Overview" > "Select Measurement" > "EMI" > "EMI Config"

On the R&S FSW, EMI measurement configuration consists of the following settings.

  • "Res BW CISPR"
  • "Res BW MIL"
  • "Bandwidth, Filter and Sweep Settings"
  • "Select Marker"
  • "Marker Settings"
  • "Demodulating Marker Values and Providing Audio Output (Marker Demodulation)"

In addition, some common settings are also relevant for EMI measurements:

  • "Transducer Settings"
  • "Limit Line Settings and Functions"
