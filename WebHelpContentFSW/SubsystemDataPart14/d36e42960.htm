CONFigure:CDPower:CTABle:MSHift <MAShift>

This command defines the number of midamble shifts in the channel table.

This value replaces the value defined by [SENSe:​]CDPower:​MSHift.

Parameters:
<numeric value>
2 | 4 | 6 | 8 | 10 | 12 | 14 | 16
*RST:16
Example: CONF:CDP:CTAB:MSH 4

Sets the number of midamble shifts to 4.

Manual operation: See "MA Shifts Cell"
See "Midamble Shift"
