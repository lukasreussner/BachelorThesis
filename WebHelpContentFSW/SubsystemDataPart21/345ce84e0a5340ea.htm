CONFigure:US:CHANnel:SBPower:UNIT:POWer <SBPUnit>

Defines whether power results are provided as absolute values (dBm) or relative to the carrier (dBc).

Parameters:
<SBPUnit>
ABSolute | RELative
*RST:RELative
Example: CONF:US:CHAN:SBP:UNIT:POW ABS
Manual operation: See "Power Unit"
