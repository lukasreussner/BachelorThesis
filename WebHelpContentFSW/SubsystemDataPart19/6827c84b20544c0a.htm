WLAN application (R&S FSW-K91) > Remote Commands for WLAN 802.11 Measurements > Configuring the WLAN IQ Measurement (Modulation Accuracy, Flatness and Tolerance) > Signal Capturing > MIMO Capture Settings > CONFigure:WLAN:ANTMatrix:ANTenna<ant>
CONFigure:WLAN:ANTMatrix:ANTenna<ant> <Antenna>

This remote control command specifies the antenna assignment of the receive path.

Suffix: 
<ant>
1..n

Analyzer

Parameters:
<Antenna>
ANTenna1 | ANTenna2 | ANTenna3 | ANTenna4 | ANTenna5 | ANTenna6 | ANTenna7 | ANTenna8 | ANT1 | ANT2 | ANT3 | ANT4 | ANT5 | ANT6 | ANT7 | ANT8

Antenna assignment of the receiver path

Example: CONF:WLAN:ANTM:ANT2 ANT1

Analyzer number 2 measures antenna no. 1

CONF:WLAN:ANTM;ANT4 ANT2

Analyzer number 42 measures antenna no. 2

Manual operation: See "Assignment"
