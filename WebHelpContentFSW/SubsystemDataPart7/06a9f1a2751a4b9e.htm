Amplifier Measurements (R&S FSW-K18) > Remote Control Commands for Amplifier Measurements > Configuring Amplifier Measurements > Synchronizing Measurement Data > CONFigure:SYNC:SOFail
CONFigure:SYNC:SOFail <State>

This command turns a measurement stop on and off, when synchronization of measured and reference signal fails.

This mostly has an effect on continuous measurements. Single measurements are not affected.

Parameters:
<State>
ON | OFF | 1 | 0
*RST:0
Example: CONF:SYNC:SOF ON

Stops the measurement when synchronization fails.

Manual operation: See "Turning synchronization of reference and measured signal on and off"
