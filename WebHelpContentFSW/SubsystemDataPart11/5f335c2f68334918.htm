Phase Deviation Time Domain

Displays the phase error of the demodulated PM signal (in rad or °) versus time.

This display requires additional option R&S FSW-K60C/-K60H.

Note: Similar to frequency deviation, the phase error is calculated for complete hops/chirps only. Thus, where no (complete) hops/chirps are available, gaps will occur in the error display.
