Visibility

Displays or hides the limit line in the diagram. Up to 8 limit lines can be visible at the same time. Inactive limit lines can also be displayed in the diagram.

Remote command: 

CALCulate<n>:​LIMit<k>:​LOWer:​STATe

CALCulate<n>:​LIMit<k>:​UPPer:​STATe

CALCulate<n>:​LIMit<k>:​ACTive?
