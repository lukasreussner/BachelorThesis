Reference Measurement

The reference measurement measures the inherent noise figure (DANL) of the R&S FSW.

To determine the inherent noise, the application performs a measurement without the signal at the input. The resulting trace shows the inherent noise of the R&S FSW only. When you substract that inherent noise from the phase noise of the measurement with trace mathematics, you get a trace that shows the phase noise of the DUT only.

Remote command: 

CONFigure:​REFMeas ONCE
