Configuring the Measurement Range

For some frequency or power calculations, it may be useful not to take the entire dwell time of the hop or length of the chirp into consideration, but only a certain range within the dwell time/length.

These settings are only available if the additional options R&S FSW-K60C/-K60H are installed.
