CONFigure:GENerator:RFOutput[:STATe] <State>

This command turns the RF output on the connected signal generator on and off.

Make sure to synchronize with *OPC? or *WAI to make sure that the command was successfully applied on the generator before sending the next command.

Parameters:
<State>
ON | OFF | 1 | 0
*RST:1
Example: 

//Turn off the RF output

CONF:GEN:RFO OFF;*WAI
Manual operation: See "Controlling the generator output state"
