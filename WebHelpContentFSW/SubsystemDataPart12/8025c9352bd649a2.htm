[SENSe:]DDEMod:PRATe <CaptOverSmplg>

Defines the number of samples that are captured per symbol, i.e. the factor by which the symbol rate is multiplied to obtain the sample rate. This parameter also affects the demodulation bandwidth and thus the usable I/Q bandwidth.

The sample rate depends on the defined "Symbol Rate" (see "Sample Rate, Symbol Rate and I/Q Bandwidth").

Setting parameters: 
<CaptOverSmplg>
2 | 4 | 8 | 16 | 32 | 64 | 128

The factor by which the symbol rate is multiplied to obtain the sample rate, e.g. 4 samples per symbol:

sample rate = 4*symbol rate

*RST:4
Example: DDEM:PRAT 8

Captures 8 points per symbol.

Manual operation: See "Sample Rate"
