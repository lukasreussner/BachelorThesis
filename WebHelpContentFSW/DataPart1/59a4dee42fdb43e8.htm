[SENSe:]LIST:RANGe<range>:BREak <State>

This command controls the sweep (for all ranges, <range> is irrelevant).

Parameters:
<State>
ON | 1

The R&S FSW stops after measuring one range.

To continue with the next range, you have to use INITiate<n>:​CONMeas.

OFF | 0

The R&S FSW sweeps all ranges in one go.

*RST:0
Manual operation: See "Stop After Sweep"
