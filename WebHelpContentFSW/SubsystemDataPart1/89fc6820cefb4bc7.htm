Spectrum

Displays the frequency spectrum of the captured I/Q samples.

The specified "Analysis Bandwidth" is indicated by vertical blue lines.

Remote command: 

LAY:ADD:WIND? '1',RIGH,FREQ, see LAYout:​ADD[:​WINDow]?

Results:

TRACe<n>[:​DATA]?
