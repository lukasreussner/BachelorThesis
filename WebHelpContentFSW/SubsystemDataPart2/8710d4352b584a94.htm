SENSe:TRACe:MEASurement:DEFine:PULSe:ADRoop <State>

If ON, a pulse can be modeled as having amplitude droop, otherwise the pulse top is assumed to be flat.

Parameters:
<State>
ON | OFF | 0 | 1
*RST:1
Manual operation: See "Pulse Has Droop"
