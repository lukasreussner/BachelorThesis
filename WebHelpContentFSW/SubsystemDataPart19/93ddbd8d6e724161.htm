WLAN application (R&S FSW-K91) > Remote Commands for WLAN 802.11 Measurements > Configuring the WLAN IQ Measurement (Modulation Accuracy, Flatness and Tolerance) > Tracking and Channel Estimation > SENSe:TRACking:IQMComp
SENSe:TRACking:IQMComp <State>

Activates or deactivates the compensation for I/Q mismatch (gain imbalance, quadrature offset, I/Q skew, see "I/Q Mismatch").

This setting is not available for standards IEEE 802.11b and g (DSSS).

Parameters:
<State>
ON | OFF | 1 | 0
ON | 1

Compensation for gain imbalance, quadrature offset, and I/Q skew impairments is applied.

OFF | 0

Compensation is not applied; this setting is required for measurements strictly according to the IEEE 802.11-2012, IEEE 802.11ac-2013 WLAN standard

*RST:0
Example: SENS:TRAC:IQMC ON
Manual operation: See "I/Q Mismatch Compensation"
