[SENSe:]DDEMod:NORMalize[:VALue] <Normalize>

This command switches the compensation of the IQ offset and the compensation of amplitude droop on or off.

Note that this command is maintained for compatibility reasons only. Use the more specific [SENSe:]DDEMod:NORMalize commands for new remote control programs (see "Demodulation Settings").

Setting parameters: 
<Normalize>
ON | OFF
OFF | 0

No compensation for amplitude droop nor I/Q offset

ON | 1

Compensation for amplitude droop and I/Q offset enabled

*RST:ON
