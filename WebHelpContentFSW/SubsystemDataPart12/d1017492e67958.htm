VSA (R&S FSW-K70) > Remote Commands for VSA > Configuring VSA > Measurement Filter Settings > [SENSe:]DDEMod:MFILter:NAME
[SENSe:]DDEMod:MFILter:NAME <Name>

This command selects a measurement filter and automatically sets its state to "ON".

Setting parameters: 
<Name>

Name of the measurement filter or 'User' for a user-defined filter.

An overview of available measurement filters is provided in "Measurement Filters".

Manual operation: See "Type"
