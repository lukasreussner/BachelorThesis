Remote commands to configure carrier aggregation

 

Remote command: 

Number of carriers: CONFigure[:​LTE]:​NOCC

Carrier frequency: [SENSe:​]FREQuency:​CENTer[:​CC<cc>]

Measurement frequency: SENSe:FREQuency:CENTer?

Offset: [SENSe]:​FREQuency:​CENTer[:​CC<cc>]:​OFFSet

Channel bandwidth: CONFigure[:​LTE]:​DL[:​CC<cc>]:​BW

Number of devices: CONFigure[:​LTE]:​NDEV

Lower adjacent channel BW: [SENSe:​]POWer:​ACHannel:​AACHannel

Upper adjacent channel BW: [SENSe]:​POWer:​ACHannel:​UAAChannel
