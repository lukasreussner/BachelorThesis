Auto Scale Once/Auto Scale Window

If enabled, both the x-axis and y-axis are automatically adapted to the current measurement results (only once, not dynamically) in the selected window.

To adapt the range of all screens together, use the "Auto Scale All" function.

Remote command: 

For statistics (result type transformation): CALCulate<n>:​STATistics:​SCALe:​AUTO ONCE

For all other results: DISPlay[:​WINDow<n>]:​TRACe<t>:​Y[:​SCALe]:​AUTO ONCE
