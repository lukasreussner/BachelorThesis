Problem: K2 and K3 cannot be calculated

Possible Solution:

K2 and K3 do not regard the "Distortion Max Frequency" parameter, but cannot be calculated if the span of the "Modulation Spectrum" display ends earlier than 2 or 3 times the fundamental frequency, respectively. Increase the demodulation bandwidth.

Note that the "AF Span" defined for the "Modulation Spectrum" result display has no effect on the THD, K2 and K3 results.
