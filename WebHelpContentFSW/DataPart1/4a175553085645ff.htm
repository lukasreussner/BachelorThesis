Channel Bandwidth

Defines the channel bandwidth on which the total power density is based. The bandwidth is positioned around the currently defined "Center Frequency".

To specify a different bandwidth for total power density calculation, or to shift the bandwidth, define an "Integration Bandwidth" manually.

Remote command: 

[SENSe:​]NPRatio:​CHANnel:​BWIDth
