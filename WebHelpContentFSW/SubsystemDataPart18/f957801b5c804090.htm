Selected Pattern for Search

Indicates which of the patterns that are assigned to the current standard is selected and will be searched for.

The selected pattern is indicated for information only and cannot be edited here, only in the "Signal Structure" settings, see "Name").

Remote command: 

[SENSe:​]DDEMod:​SEARch:​SYNC:​SELect
