PvT Falling Edge

Displays the minimum, average and maximum power vs time diagram for the falling edge of all PPDUs.

Remote command: 

LAY:ADD:WIND '2',RIGH,PFAL see LAYout:​ADD[:​WINDow]?

Or:

CONFigure:​BURSt:​PVT:​SELect

CONFigure:​BURSt:​PVT[:​IMMediate]

Querying results:

TRACe[:​DATA], see "Power Vs Time (PVT)"
