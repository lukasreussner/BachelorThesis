Abbreviations

The following abbreviations are used throughout this documentation:

Abbreviation

Description

ADC

Analog to digital converter

AF signal

Audio frequency signal. Signal used to modulate a carrier (its amplitude or frequency or phase)

AM

Amplitude modulation

BP

Band pass (filter)

CF

Center frequency

DBW

Demodulation bandwidth

DUT

Device under test

FFT

Fast Fourier transform

FM

Frequency modulation

IF

Intermediate frequency

ILS

Instrument landing system

K2, K3

The Total Harmonic Distortion figure restricted up to 2nd or 3rd harmonic; also referred to as THD2 or THD3, respectively

LP

Low pass (filter)

NCO

Numerically controlled oscillator

THD

Total harmonic distortion

VOR

VHF omnidirectional range
