INPut<ip>:COUPling <CouplingType>

This command selects the coupling type of the RF input.

Suffix: 
<ip>

irrelevant

Parameters:
<CouplingType>
AC | DC
AC

AC coupling

DC

DC coupling

*RST:AC
Example: INP:COUP DC
Manual operation: See "Input Coupling"
