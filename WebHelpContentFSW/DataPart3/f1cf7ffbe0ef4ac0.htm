Continuous Demodulation

If activated, the signal is demodulated continuously (not only at the marker position) and sent to the audio output. This allows you to monitor the frequency range acoustically (assuming the sweep time is long enough).

For zero span and EMI measurements, demodulation is always active continuously.

Remote command: 

CALCulate<n>:​MARKer<m>:​FUNCtion:​DEModulation:​CONTinuous
