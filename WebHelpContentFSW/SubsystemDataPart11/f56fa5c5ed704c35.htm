Evaluation Basis

Access: [MEAS]

Depending on the measurement task, not all of the measured data in the capture buffer may be of interest. In some cases it may be useful to restrict analysis to a specific user-definable region, or to a selected individual chirp rate or hop.

Which evaluation basis is available for which result display is indicated in Table "Available evaluation methods and evaluation basis".

Some of these settings are only available if at least one of the additional options R&S FSW-K60C/-K60H are installed.
