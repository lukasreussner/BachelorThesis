CALCulate<n>:MARKer<m>:COUNt:RESolution <Resolution>

This command defines the resolution of the frequency counter.

Suffix: 
<n>

Window

<m>

Marker

Parameters:
<Resolution>
0.001 | 0.01 | 0.1 | 1 | 10 | 100 | 1000 | 10000 Hz
*RST:0.1 Hz
Example: CALC:MARK:COUN:RES 1kHz

Sets the resolution of the frequency counter to 1 kHz.

Manual operation: See "Resolution"
