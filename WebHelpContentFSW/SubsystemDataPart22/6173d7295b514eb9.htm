CONFigure[:LTE]:DL[:CC<cc>]:PHICh:DURation <Duration>

This command selects the PHICH duration.

Suffix: 
<cc>

Component Carrier

Parameters:
<Duration>
NORM

Normal

EXT

Extended

*RST:NORM
Example: CONF:DL:PHIC:DUR NORM

Selects normal PHICH duration.

Manual operation: See "PHICH Duration"
