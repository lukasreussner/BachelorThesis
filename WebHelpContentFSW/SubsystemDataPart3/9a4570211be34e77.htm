AF Auto Scale

Activates automatic scaling of the y-axis for AF measurements. RF power and RF spectrum measurements are not affected by the auto-scaling.

Remote command: 

[SENSe:​]ADJust:​SCALe[:​Y]:​AUTO[:​CONTinuous]
