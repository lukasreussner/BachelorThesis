[SENSe:]PMETer<p>[:STATe] <State>

This command turns a power sensor on and off.

Suffix: 
<p>
1...4

Power sensor index

Parameters:
<State>
ON | OFF | 1 | 0
*RST:0
Example: PMET1 ON

Switches the power sensor measurements on.
