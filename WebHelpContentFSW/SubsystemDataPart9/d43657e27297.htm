CONFigure:REFMeas ONCE

This command initiates a reference measurement that determines the inherent phase noise of the R&S FSW.

Parameters:
ONCE
Example: CONF:REFM ONCE

Initiates a reference measurement

Manual operation: See "Reference Measurement"
