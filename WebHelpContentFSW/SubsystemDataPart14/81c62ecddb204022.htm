No of Subframes

Defines the number of subframes that the R&S FSW includes in the measurement. The results of the Power vs Time measurement are based on the average of the number of the subframes. This setting is identical to the "Sweep/Average Count".

Remote command: 

CONFigure:​CDPower[:​BTS]:​PVTime:​SFRames
