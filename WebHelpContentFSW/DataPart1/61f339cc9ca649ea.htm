Deactivating and Activating the Touchscreen

The touchscreen function can be deactivated, e.g. when the instrument is being used for demonstration purposes and tapping the screen must not provoke an action.

To reactivate the touchscreen, simply press the SETUP key on the front panel. The "Display" dialog box is opened automatically and the "Touch Screen" option is set to "On".

"Touch On"

Touchscreen function is active for the entire screen.

"Touch Off"

Touchscreen is deactivated for the entire screen.

"Touch Diagram Off"

Touchscreen is deactivated for the diagram area of the screen, but active for the surrounding softkeys, toolbars and menus.

Remote command: 

DISPlay:​TOUChscreen[:​STATe]
