Time Resolution

The time resolution determines the size of the bins used for each FFT calculation. The shorter the time span used for each FFT, the shorter the resulting span, and thus the higher the resolution in the spectrum becomes.

In "Auto" mode, the optimal resolution is determined automatically according to the data acquisition settings.

In "Manual" mode, you must define the time resolution in seconds.

For details on the FFT calculation see "Signal Processing".

Remote command: 

CALCulate<n>:​SGRam:​TRESolution:​AUTO

CALCulate<n>:​SGRam:​TRESolution
