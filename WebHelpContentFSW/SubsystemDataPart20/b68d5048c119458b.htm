Numeric Results for SEM Measurements

The following commands are required to retrieve the numeric results of the IEEE 802.11ad SEM measurements (see "SEM Measurements".

In the following commands used to retrieve the numeric results for RF data, the suffixes <n> for CALCulate and <k> for LIMit are irrelevant.

List of Commands
CALCulate<n>:LIMit<li>:FAIL?
CALCulate<n>:MARKer<m>:X
