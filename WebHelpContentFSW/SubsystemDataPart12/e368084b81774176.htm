Display Lines for Eye Diagrams

For eye diagrams (see "Result Types in VSA"), display lines allow you to measure the size of the eye in the diagram. For other result displays, lines are not available.

Access: [LINES] > "Lines/Limits"

For a detailed description on the use of display lines in eye diagrams, see "How to Measure the Size of an Eye".
