Configuration

Access: MODE > "Docsis 3.1"

The default DOCSIS 3.1 I/Q measurement captures the I/Q data from the DOCSIS 3.1 signal and determines various characteristic signal parameters such as the modulation accuracy, spectrum flatness, center frequency tolerance and symbol clock tolerance in just one measurement (see "Measurements and Result Display").

Other parameters specified in the DOCSIS 3.1 standard must be determined in separate measurements (see "Frequency Sweep Measurements").

The settings required to configure each of these measurements are described here.
