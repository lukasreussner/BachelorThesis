CALCulate<n>:MARKer<m>:LINK <State>

This command turns marker coupling across result displays on and off.

Suffix: 
<n>

irrelevant

<m>

irrelevant

Parameters:
<State>
ON | OFF | 1 | 0
*RST:OFF
Example: 

//Couple markers

CALC:MARK:LINK ON
