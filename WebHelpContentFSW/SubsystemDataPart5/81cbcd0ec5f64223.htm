Harmonic Frequency

Defines the frequency at which the harmonic distortion is measured.

Tip: the fixed "H1" marker in the "Modulation Spectrum" display indicates the distortion at the given frequency relative to the modulation at the set "Fundamental Frequency".

Remote command: 

CALCulate<n>:​AVIonics:​SHD:​FREQuency
