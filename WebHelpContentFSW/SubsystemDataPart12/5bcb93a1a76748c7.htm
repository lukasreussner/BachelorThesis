Marker Search and Positioning Settings

Several functions are available to set the marker to a specific position very quickly and easily. In order to determine the required marker position, searches may be performed. The search results can be influenced by special settings.

Useful commands for positioning markers described elsewhere:
  • CALCulate<n>:​MARKer<m>:​TRACe
  • CALCulate<n>:​DELTamarker<m>:​TRACe
