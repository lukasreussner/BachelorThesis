Trigger Settings

Access: "Overview" > "Signal Capture" > "Trigger Source"

Trigger settings determine when the R&S FSW starts to capture the input signal.

External triggers from one of the [TRIGGER INPUT/OUTPUT] connectors on the R&S FSW are also available.

See Trigger Input/Output Settings.

For more information on trigger settings see "Triggered Measurements".

For step-by-step instructions on configuring triggered measurements, see How to Configure a Triggered Measurement.
