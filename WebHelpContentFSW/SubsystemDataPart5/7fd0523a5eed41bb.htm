[SENSe:]ADEMod:SPECtrum:BANDwidth[:RESolution]:AUTO <DemodResBWAuto>
[SENSe:]ADEMod:SPECtrum:BWIDth[:RESolution]:AUTO <DemodResBWAuto>

This command defines whether the resolution bandwidth for "Modulation Spectrum" results is determined automatically or not.

Parameters:
<DemodResBWAuto>
ON | OFF | 1 | 0
ON | 1

The RBW is determined automatically.

OFF | 0

The RBW is defined by the [SENSe:​]ADEMod:​SPECtrum:​BWIDth[:​RESolution] command.

*RST:1
Example: SENS:ADEM:SPEC:BWID:AUTO OFF
SENS:ADEM:SPEC:BWID 2.5HZ
Manual operation: See "RBW"
