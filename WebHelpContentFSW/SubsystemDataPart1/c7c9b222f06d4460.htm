Alignment

Performs an alignment of the B2000/B5000 connection on the R&S FSW.

After alignment was performed successfully, the date of alignment is indicated.

If [UNCAL] is displayed, alignment was not yet performed (successfully).

Note: If the oscilloscope is detected to be uncalibrated, a self-alignment is performed on the oscilloscope before the actual B5000 alignment starts. This may take a few minutes.
In some cases, it is useful to force a self-alignment on the oscilloscope manually, see "Force Oscilloscope Alignment".

Remote command: 

SYSTem:​COMMunicate:​RDEVice:​OSCilloscope:​ALIGnment:​STEP[:​STATe]?

SYSTem:​COMMunicate:​RDEVice:​OSCilloscope:​ALIGnment:​DATE?
