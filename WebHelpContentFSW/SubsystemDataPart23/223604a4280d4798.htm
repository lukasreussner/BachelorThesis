[SENSe:][LTE:]SFLatness:ECONditions <State>

This command turns extreme conditions for spectrum flatness measurements on and off.

Parameters:
<State>
ON | OFF | 1 | 0
*RST:OFF
Example: 

//Turn on extreme conditions

SFL:ECON ON
Manual operation: See "Extreme Conditions"
