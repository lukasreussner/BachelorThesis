HCOPy:PAGE:MARGin<device>:TOP <Margin>

This command defines the margin at the top of the printout page on which no elements are printed. The margins are defined according to HCOPy:​PAGE:​MARGin<device>:​UNIT.

Suffix: 
<device>
1 | 2

Printing device.

Parameters:
<Margin>
numeric value
*RST:4.23 mm
Example: HCOP:PAGE:MARG2:TOP 2
Manual operation: See "Margins"
