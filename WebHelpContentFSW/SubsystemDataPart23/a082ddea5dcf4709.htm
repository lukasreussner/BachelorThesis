SRS Rel Power

Defines the power of the SRS relative to the power of the corresponding UE (PSRS_Offset).

The effective power level of the SRS is calculated as follows.

PSRS = PUE + PSRS_Offset

The relative power of the SRS is applied to all subframes.

Remote command: 

CONFigure[:​LTE]:​UL[:​CC<cc>]:​SRS:​POWer
