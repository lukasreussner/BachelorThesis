Display Style

Determines the trace style for the parameter trend.

"Dots"

Each result is displayed as a dot in the diagram.

"Lines"

The results are connected by lines in the diagram.

"Both"

Both the dots for individual results and the connecting lines are displayed in the diagram.

"Auto"

The optimal display style is determined automatically depending on the parameter type on the x-axis.

For chronological (time-based) parameters (pulse number or pulse timestamp), lines are displayed.

For all other parameters, dots are displayed.

Remote command: 

CALCulate<n>:​TRENd:​DSTYle
