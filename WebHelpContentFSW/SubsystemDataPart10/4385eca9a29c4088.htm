Transfer to Wide Search Settings

Stores all segment settings in the current "Segment" table to the "Wide Search Settings" table. Settings for individual segments cannot be transferred separately. The "Auto RBW" setting is deactivated.

Remote command: 

[SENSe:​]TRANsfer:​SEGMent
