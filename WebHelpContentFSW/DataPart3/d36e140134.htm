[SENSe:]LIST:RANGe<range>:DELete

This command removes a range from the sweep list.

Note that

  • you cannot delete the reference range
  • a minimum of three ranges is mandatory.
Suffix: 
<range>
1..30

Selects the measurement range.

Usage: Event
