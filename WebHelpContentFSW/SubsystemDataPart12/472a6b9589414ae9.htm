Burst Search

Access: "Overview" > "Burst/Pattern" > "Burst Search"

or: [MEAS CONFIG] > "Burst/Pattern Search" > "Burst Search" tab

The "Burst Search" settings define when a burst is detected in the evaluated signal.

A live preview of the capture buffer with the current settings is displayed in the preview area at the bottom of the dialog box. The blue lines below the trace indicate the detected bursts. The preview area is not editable directly.

For details on burst see "Overview of the Demodulation Process".
