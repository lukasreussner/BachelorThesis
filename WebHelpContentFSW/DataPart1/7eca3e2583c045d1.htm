Fast ACLR

If activated, instead of using the IBW method, the R&S FSW sets the center frequency to the different channel center frequencies consecutively and measures the power with the selected measurement time (= sweep time/number of channels).

For details see "Measurement Methods".

Remote command: 

[SENSe:​]POWer:​HSPeed
