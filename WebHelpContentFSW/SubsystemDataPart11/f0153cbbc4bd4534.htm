Min Dwell Time / Max Dwell Time

If "Auto Mode" is disabled, you can define minimum or maximum dwell times, or both, manually, in order to detect only specific hops, for example.

Remote command: 

CALCulate<n>:​CHRDetection:​LENGth:​MAXimum

CALCulate<n>:​CHRDetection:​LENGth:​MINimum

CALCulate<n>:​HOPDetection:​DWELl:​MAXimum

CALCulate<n>:​HOPDetection:​DWELl:​MINimum
