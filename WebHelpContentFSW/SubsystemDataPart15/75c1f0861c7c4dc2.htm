Spectrum Emission Mask

Access: "Overview" > "Select Measurement" > "Spectrum Emission Mask"

The Spectrum Emission Mask measurement determines the power of the CDMA2000 signal in defined offsets from the carrier and compares the power values with a spectral mask specified by the CDMA2000 specifications. The limits depend on the selected bandclass. Thus, the performance of the DUT can be tested and the emissions and their distance to the limit be identified.

Note: The CDMA2000 standard does not distinguish between spurious and spectral emissions.

For details, see "Spectrum Emission Mask".
