Bandclass

The bandclass defines the frequency band used for ACLR and SEM measurements. It also determines the corresponding limits and ACLR channel settings according to the CDMA2000 standard.

For an overview of supported bandclasses and their usage, see "Reference: Supported Bandclasses".

Remote command: 

CONFigure:​CDPower[:​BTS]:​BCLass|BANDclass
