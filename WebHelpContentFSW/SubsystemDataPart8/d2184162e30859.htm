[SENSe:]FREQuency:SINGle <Frequency>

This command defines the frequency for single frequency measurements.

Parameters:
<Frequency>

The minimum and maximum frequency depend on the hardware. Refer to the datasheet for details.

*RST:100 MHz
Example: FREQ:SING 200MHZ

Defines a measurement frequency of 200 MHz.
