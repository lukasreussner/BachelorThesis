Range Start / Range Stop

Sets the start frequency/stop frequency of the selected range.

Follow the rules described in "Ranges and Range Settings".

You can define a span that is smaller than the overall span of the ranges. In this case, the measurement includes only the ranges that lie within the defined span and have a minimum span of 20 Hz.

Remote command: 

[SENSe:​]LIST:​RANGe<range>[:​FREQuency]:​STARt

[SENSe:​]LIST:​RANGe<range>[:​FREQuency]:​STOP
