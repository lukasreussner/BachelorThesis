Scrambling of Coded Bits

Turns the scrambling of coded bits for all physical channels like PDSCH or PHICH on and off.

The scrambling of coded bits affects the bitstream results.

Source for bitstream results if scrambling for coded bits is on and off
Remote command: 

[SENSe:​][LTE:​]DL:​DEMod:​CBSCrambling
