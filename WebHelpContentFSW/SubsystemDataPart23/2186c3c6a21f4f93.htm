Modulation

Selects the modulation scheme for the corresponding PUSCH allocation.

The modulation scheme is either QPSK, 16QAM, 64QAM or 256QAM.

Remote command: 

CONFigure[:​LTE]:​UL[:​CC<cc>]:​SUBFrame<sf>:​ALLoc:​MODulation
