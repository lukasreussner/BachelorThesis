DISPlay[:WINDow<n>]:TDOMain:X[:SCALe]:MODE <State>

This command turns automatic scaling of the x-axis in the Time Domain result display on and off.

Suffix: 
<n>

Window

Parameters:
<State>
ON | 1

Turns on automatic scaling of the x-axis.

OFF | 0

Turns on manual scaling of the x-axis.

Example: DISP:TDOM:X:MODE OFF

Turns on manual scaling of the x-axis.

Manual operation: See "Configuring the Time Domain result display"
