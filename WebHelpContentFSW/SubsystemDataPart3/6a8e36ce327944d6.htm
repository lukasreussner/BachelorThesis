[SENSe:]BANDwidth:DEMod <Bandwidth>
[SENSe:]BWIDth:DEMod <Bandwidth>

This command sets the bandwidth for analog demodulation. Depending on the selected demodulation bandwidth, the instrument selects the required sample rate.

For details on the correlation between demodulation bandwidth and sample rate refer to "Sample Rate and Demodulation Bandwidth".

This command is identical to SENS:ADEM:BAND:DEM.

Parameters:
<Bandwidth>
*RST:5 MHz
Example: 

BAND:DEM 1MHz

Sets demodulation bandwidth to 1 MHz
