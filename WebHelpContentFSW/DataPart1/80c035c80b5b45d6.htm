Transducer Factor

Sets a transducer for the specified range. You can only choose a transducer that fulfills the following conditions:

  • The transducer overlaps or equals the span of the range.
  • The x-axis is linear.
  • The unit is dB.

For details on transducers, see "Basics on Transducer Factors".

Remote command: 

[SENSe:​]ESPectrum<sb>:​RANGe<range>:​TRANsducer
