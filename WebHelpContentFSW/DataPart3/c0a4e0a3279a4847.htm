Remote Commands > Configuring the R&S FSW > Remote Commands for Language Settings > SYSTem:DISPlay:LANGuage
SYSTem:DISPlay:LANGuage <Language>

Defines the language of the software-defined interface elements (such as softkeys, dialog boxes, diagram texts etc.).

Parameters:
<Language>
'EN' | 'ZH_CH' | 'ZH_TW' | 'JA' | 'KO' | 'RU'
'ZH_CH'

Simplified Chinese

'ZH_TW'

Traditional Chinese

*RST:'EN'
Example: SYST:DISP:LANG 'JA'

Switches the language of the instrument to Japanese.
