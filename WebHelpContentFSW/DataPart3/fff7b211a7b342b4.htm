CALCulate<n>:MARKer<m>:FUNCtion:TOI:SEARchsignal ONCE

This command initiates a search for signals in the current trace to determine the third intercept point.

Suffix: 
<n>, <m>

irrelevant

Parameters:
ONCE
Example: CALC:MARK:FUNC:TOI:SEAR ONCE

Executes the search for 2 signals and their intermodulation product at the currently available trace.

See "Programming Example: Measuring the TOI".

Usage: Event
