CONFigure[:MS]:CHANnel:SLOT<Number>:FILTer <Type>

This command specifies the pulse shape of the ideal modulator.

Suffix: 
<Number>
<0..7>

the slot to configure

Parameters for setting and query: 
<Type>
GMSK | LINearised | NARRow | WIDE
GMSK

GMSK Pulse

LINearised

Linearised GMSK Pulse

NARRow

Narrow Pulse

WIDE
