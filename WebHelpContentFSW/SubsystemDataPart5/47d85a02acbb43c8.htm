ILS Parameters

For ILS measurements, the following parameters are determined.

└ 90 Hz AM frequency
└ 90 Hz AM THD

└ 150 Hz AM frequency
└ 150 Hz AM THD
└ 90+150 Hz AM depth
└ 90+150 Hz AM phase
└ 90+150 Hz AM THD
└ Voice / IDENT AM depth
└ Voice / IDENT AM frequency
└ Voice / IDENT AM THD
└ Ident Code
└ SDM
└ ILS DDM
└ K2
└ K3
└ THD total
