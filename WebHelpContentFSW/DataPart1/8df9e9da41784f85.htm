Electromagnetic Interference (EMI) Measurement

The optional electromagnetic interference (EMI) measurement (R&S FSW-K54) is suitable for measurements according to commercial and military electromagnetic compatibility (EMC) standards. The functionality of the measurement is particularly useful in research and development.

The EMI measurement features:

  • EMI marker functionality
  • Marker demodulation
  • Measurement bandwidths and detectors for EMI measurements
  • Logarithmic scaling of the frequency axis
  • Additional predefined limit lines for EMC standards
  • Predefined transducer factors
  • LISN control
