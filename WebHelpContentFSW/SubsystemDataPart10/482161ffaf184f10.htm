[SENSe:]LIST:RANGe<range>:BANDwidth[:RESolution] <RBW>

This command defines the resolution bandwidth for a spurious emission measurement range.

Suffix: 
<range>
1..n

Measurement range

Parameters:
<RBW>
Range: 1 Hz to 10 MHz
*RST:3 MHz
Default unit: HZ
Manual operation: See "RBW"
