Detector

Detector type for demodulation results

"+ Peak"

Positive peak

"- Peak"

Negative peak

"+/- Peak"

Autopeak

"RMS"

Root mean square

Remote command: 

The detector is specified by the DETector<det> suffix in CONFigure:RELative:AM|FM|PM:DETector<det>... commands.
