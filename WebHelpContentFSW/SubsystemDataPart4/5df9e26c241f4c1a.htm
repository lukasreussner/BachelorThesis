Individual Marker Settings

In GSM evaluations, up to 4 markers can be activated in each diagram at any time. the following commandas are required to configure the markers.

List of Commands
CALCulate<n>:DELTamarker<m>:AOFF
CALCulate<n>:DELTamarker<m>[:STATe]
CALCulate<n>:DELTamarker<m>:TRACe
CALCulate<n>:MARKer<m>[:STATe]
CALCulate<n>:MARKer<m>:AOFF
CALCulate<n>:MARKer<m>:TRACe
