Multicarrier Filter

The "Multicarrier" filter is a special filter that is applied to the captured I/Q data if the device is defined as a multicarrier type (see "Device Type"). This filter is used to suppress neighboring channels which may disturb measurement of the channel of interest. The output from the "Multicarrier" filter is used to perform synchronization and demodulation.
