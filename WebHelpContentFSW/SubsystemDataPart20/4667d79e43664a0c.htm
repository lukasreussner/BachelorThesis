Starting a Measurement

When a IEEE 802.11ad measurement channel is activated on the R&S FSW, a IEEE 802.11ad Modulation Accuracy Measurement, see "IEEE 802.11ad Modulation Accuracy Measurement"), is started immediately. However, you can stop and start a new measurement any time.

Furthermore, you can perform a sequence of measurements using the Sequencer (see "Multiple Measurement Channels and Sequencer Function").
