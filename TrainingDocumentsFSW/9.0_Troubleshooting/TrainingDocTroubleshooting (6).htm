Power levels for low frequency signals not correct

By default, the R&S FSW uses AC coupling for RF input. For very low frequencies, the input signal may be distorted with this setting. In this case, use DC coupling instead. To change the setting, select INPUT/OUPUT > "Input Source Config > Radio Frequency > Input Coupling > DC".
