Band Category

Defines the band category for MSR measurements, i.e. the combination of available carriers to measure.

For details, see "Multi-Standard Radio (MSR) SEM Measurements".

"BC1"

LTE FDD and W-CDMA

"BC2"

LTE FDD, W-CDMA and GSM/EDGE

"BC3"

LTE TDD and TD-SCDMA

Remote command: 

[SENSe:​]ESPectrum<sb>:​MSR:​BCATegory
