Multi-Carrier Group Delay (R&S FSW-K17) > Remote Commands to Perform Multi-Carrier Group Delay Measurements > Configuring Multi-Carrier Group Delay Measurements > Configuring the Vertical Axis (Amplitude, Scaling) > Attenuation > INPut<ip>:EATT:AUTO
INPut<ip>:EATT:AUTO <State>

This command turns automatic selection of the electronic attenuation on and off.

If on, electronic attenuation reduces the mechanical attenuation whenever possible.

Suffix: 
<ip>
1 | 2

irrelevant

Parameters:
<State>
ON | OFF | 0 | 1
OFF | 0

Switches the function off

ON | 1

Switches the function on

*RST:1
Example: INP:EATT:AUTO OFF
Manual operation: See "Using Electronic Attenuation"
