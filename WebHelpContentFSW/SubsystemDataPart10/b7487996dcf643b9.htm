Minimum Spur SNR

Defines the minimum signal-to-noise ratio (in dB) that the spur should be displayed with after the measurement is finished (see "Measurement Process"). The required RBW to achieve this SNR is determined automatically by the R&S FSW Spurious measurements application.

Note: If the minimum SNR is not achieved for a spur during the spurious detection measurement, the RBW is reduced during the spot search. Thus, the noise drops in a small span around the spur.

Remote command: 

[SENSe:​]LIST:​RANGe<range>:​SNRatio
