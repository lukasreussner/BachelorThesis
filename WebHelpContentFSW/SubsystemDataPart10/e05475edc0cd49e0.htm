Search Span

Defines the span around the frequency for which a detailed measurement (spurious detection sweep and spur frequency scan, see "Measurement Process") is performed.

Note that the frequency spans must be distinct, that is: they may not overlap.

Remote command: 

[SENSe:​]DIRected:​SETTings
