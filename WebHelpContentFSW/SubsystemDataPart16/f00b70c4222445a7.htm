Power

Access: "Overview" > "Select Measurement" > "Power"

The Power measurement determines the 1xEV-DO signal channel power.

To do so, the 1xEV-DO application performs a Channel Power measurement as in the Spectrum application with settings according to the 1xEV-DO standard. The bandwidth and the associated channel power are displayed in the Result Summary.

For details see "Signal Channel Power Measurements".

Remote command: 

CONF:CDP:MEAS POW, see CONFigure:​CDPower[:​BTS]:​MEASurement

Querying results: CALC:MARK:FUNC:POW:RES? CPOW, see CALCulate<n>:​MARKer<m>:​FUNCtion:​POWer<sb>:​RESult?

CALC:MARK:FUNC:POW:RES? ACP, see CALCulate<n>:​MARKer<m>:​FUNCtion:​POWer<sb>:​RESult?
