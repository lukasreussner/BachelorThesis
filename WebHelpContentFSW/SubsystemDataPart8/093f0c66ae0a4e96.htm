CALCulate:UNCertainty:ENR:CALibration:UNCertainty:HOT <Uncertainty>

This command defines the uncertainty of a calibration noise source.

This command is available when [SENSe:]CORRection:ENR:COMMon and CALCulate:UNCertainty:COMMon are off.

Parameters:
<Uncertainty>

Hot temperature uncertainty value of the noise source.

Refer to the data sheet of the noise source to determine its uncertainty.

*RST:0 K
Example: CALC:UNC:ENR:CAL:UNC:COLD 5 K

Defines a high temperature uncertainty of 5 K.

Manual operation: See "Temperature Uncert(ainty)"
