Reference: List of Available RRC and Channel Filters

For power measurement a number of especially steep-edged channel filters are available (see the following table). The indicated filter bandwidth is the 3 dB bandwidth.

For RRC filters, the fixed roll-off factor (α) is also indicated.

The available Gaussian 3 dB sweep filters are listed in the R&S FSW data sheet.
