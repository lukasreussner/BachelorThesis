Trace Evaluation

Traces in graphical result displays based on the defined result range (see "Result Range") can be configured, for example to perform statistical evaluations over the selected hop/chirp or all hops/chirps.

You can configure up to 6 individual traces for the following result displays (see "Evaluation Methods for Transient Analysis"):
  • "RF Power Time Domain"
  • "FM Time Domain"
  • "Frequency Deviation Time Domain"
  • "PM Time Domain"
  • "PM Time Domain (Wrapped)"
  • "Chirp Rate Time Domain"
