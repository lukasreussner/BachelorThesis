Sample Rate

Defines the I/Q data sample rate of the R&S FSW. This value is dependent on the defined "Analysis Bandwidth" and the defined signal source.

Up to the "Maximum Bandwidth", the following rule applies:

sample rate = analysis bandwidth / 0.8

For details on the dependencies see "Sample Rate and Maximum Usable I/Q Bandwidth for RF Input".

In particular, note the irregularities mentioned in "R&S FSW with Activated I/Q Bandwidth Extension Option -B512" and "Irregular behavior in bandwidths between 480 MHz and 512 MHz with R&S FSW-B1200 option".

This rate may differ from the sample rate of the connected device (see "Input Sample Rate").

If the Digital Baseband Interface (R&S FSW-B17) is active, restrictions to the sample rate apply, see "Sample Rates and Bandwidths for Digital I/Q Data".

Remote command: 

TRACe:​IQ:​SRATe
