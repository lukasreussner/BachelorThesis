YIG-Preselector

Activates or deactivates the YIG-preselector, if available on the R&S FSW.

Note that the YIG-preselector is active only on frequencies greater than 8 GHz. Therefore, switching the YIG-preselector on or off has no effect if the frequency is below that value.

In order to make use of the optional 90 GHz frequency extension (R&S FSW-B90G), the YIG-preselector must be deactivated.

Remote command: 

INPut<ip>:​FILTer:​YIG[:​STATe]
