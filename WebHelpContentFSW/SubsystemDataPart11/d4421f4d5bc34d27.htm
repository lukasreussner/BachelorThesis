Instead of one hop/chirp, several shorter hop/chirps of a different hop/chirp state are detected

Adjust the detection tolerance of the corresponding hop/chirp states to make sure that tolerance ranges do not overlap (see "Tolerance").

Use a video filter with a smaller VBW (see "FM Video Bandwidth").
