CONFigure:WLAN:RUConfig:COUNt:HIGHest?

Queries the highest configured RU index in the entire signal.

Return values: 
<MaxIndex>
Range: 1 to 9
Example: CONF:WLAN:RUC:COUN:HIGH?
Usage: Query only
