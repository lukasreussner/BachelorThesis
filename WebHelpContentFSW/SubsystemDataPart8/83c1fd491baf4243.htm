ENR Uncert(ainty)

Defines the uncertainty of the excess noise ratio of the noise source you are using.

Refer to the datasheet of the noise source for this value.

Available for noise sources with diode characteristics.

Remote command: 

CALCulate:​UNCertainty:​ENR:​UNCertainty

CALCulate:​UNCertainty:​ENR:​CALibration:​UNCertainty
