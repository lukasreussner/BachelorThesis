Signal Bandwidth

Defines the bandwidth of the reference signal.

The bandwidth should not be larger than maximum I/Q bandwidth supported by your signal analyzer (which depends on the analyzer configuration).

Remote command: 

CONFigure:​REFSignal:​GOS:​BWIDth
