CALCulate<n>:DELTamarker<m>:FUNCtion:FMEasurement:DETector <Detector>

This command selects the detector for a specific deltamarker during the final evaluation.

If the deltamarker is not yet active, the command also turns the deltamarker on.

Suffix: 
<n>

irrelevant

<m>

Marker

Parameters:
<Detector>
OFF

no final measurement is performed

AVER

average detector

CAV

CISPR Average detector

CRMS

RMS Average detector

POS

maximum peak detector

QPE

quasipeak detector

*RST:OFF
Manual operation: See "Final Test Detector"
