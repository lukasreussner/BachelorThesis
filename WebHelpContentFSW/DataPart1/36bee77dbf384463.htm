[SENSe:]POWer:BWIDth <Percentage>
[SENSe:]POWer:BANDwidth <Percentage>

This command selects the percentage of the total power that defines the occupied bandwidth.

Parameters:
<Percentage>
Range: 10 PCT  to  99.9 PCT
*RST:99 PCT
Example: POW:BAND 95PCT
Example: 

See "Programming Example: OBW Measurement".
