Clock Rate

Defines the clock or sample rate that the reference signal is generated with.

The purpose of the application is to measure nonlinear effects. These generate spectral regrowth (amplitude components in addition to the signal).

Remote command: 

CONFigure:​REFSignal:​GOS:​SRATe
