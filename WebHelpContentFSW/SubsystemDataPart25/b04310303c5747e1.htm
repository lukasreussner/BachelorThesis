Multicarrier Filter

Turns the suppression of interference of neighboring carriers for tests on multiradio base stations on and off (e.g. LTE, WCDMA, GSM etc.).

Remote command: 

[SENSe:​][LTE:​]DL:​DEMod:​MCFilter
